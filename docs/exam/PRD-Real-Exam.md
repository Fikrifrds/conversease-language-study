# Product Requirements Document: Conversease Real Exam

## 1. Overview

### 1.1 Vision
Menyediakan standardized English proficiency assessment yang terintegrasi dengan learning journey Conversease, memungkinkan user mengukur kemajuan mereka dari A1 hingga C1 secara objektif dan reliable.

### 1.2 Target User
- **Primary**: Learners yang ingin mendapatkan sertifikasi level completion
- **Secondary**: Instructors/admin yang perlu menilai kemajuan learner
- **Tertiary**: HR/recruiters yang menggunakan Conversease sebagai assessment tool

### 1.3 Success Metrics
- **Adoption**: 80% learners mengambil exam setelah menyelesaikan 80% lessons per level
- **Reliability**: Test-retest reliability > 0.85
- **Validity**: Correlation dengan standardized tests (IELTS/TOEFL) > 0.75
- **User Satisfaction**: NPS > 50 untuk exam experience
- **Admin Efficiency**: < 5 menit rata-rata review time per speaking/writing section

## 2. Exam Structure

### 2.1 Section Breakdown per Level

#### A1 (Beginner) - Duration: ~45 menit
| Section | Items | Weight | Format | Time |
|---------|-------|--------|--------|------|
| Listening | 8-10 items | 25% | Audio + MCQ/short answer | 10 min |
| Reading | 5-7 items | 20% | Passage + MCQ/fill-in | 8 min |
| Grammar & Vocabulary | 10-12 items | 20% | MCQ, matching, sentence completion | 10 min |
| Speaking | 3-4 tasks | 25% | Recorded audio responses | 5 min |
| Writing | 1-2 tasks | 10% | Short text (30-50 words) | 2 min |

#### A2 (Elementary) - Duration: ~60 menit
| Section | Items | Weight | Format | Time |
|---------|-------|--------|--------|------|
| Listening | 10-12 items | 25% | Audio + MCQ/short answer/matching | 12 min |
| Reading | 6-8 items | 20% | 2 short passages + MCQ/fill-in | 10 min |
| Grammar & Vocabulary | 12-15 items | 20% | MCQ, error correction, sentence transformation | 12 min |
| Speaking | 4-5 tasks | 25% | Recorded audio responses | 7 min |
| Writing | 2 tasks | 10% | Short messages/emails (50-80 words) | 5 min |

#### B1 (Intermediate) - Duration: ~75 menit
| Section | Items | Weight | Format | Time |
|---------|-------|--------|--------|------|
| Listening | 12-15 items | 25% | 2-3 audios + MCQ/multiple matching/note completion | 15 min |
| Reading | 8-10 items | 20% | 2 passages (different text types) + MCQ/gapped text/matching | 15 min |
| Grammar & Vocabulary | 15-18 items | 15% | MCQ, key word transformation, error correction, collocation | 15 min |
| Speaking | 5-6 tasks | 25% | Recorded audio responses with longer turns | 10 min |
| Writing | 2 tasks | 15% | Essay (150-200 words) + formal/informal writing task | 10 min |

#### B2 (Upper-Intermediate) - Duration: ~90 menit
| Section | Items | Weight | Format | Time |
|---------|-------|--------|--------|------|
| Listening | 15-18 items | 25% | 3-4 audios + multiple matching/note completion/sentence completion | 18 min |
| Reading | 10-12 items | 20% | 3 passages (complex/abstract) + MCQ/gapped text/multiple matching | 18 min |
| Use of English | 18-20 items | 15% | MCQ, open cloze, word formation, key word transformation | 15 min |
| Speaking | 6-7 tasks | 25% | Extended discourse tasks, argumentation, comparison | 12 min |
| Writing | 2 tasks | 15% | Essay (200-250 words) + proposal/report/review/article | 12 min |

#### C1 (Advanced) - Duration: ~105 menit
| Section | Items | Weight | Format | Time |
|---------|-------|--------|--------|------|
| Listening | 18-22 items | 25% | 4-5 audios + complex multiple matching/attitude/opinion identification | 22 min |
| Reading | 12-14 items | 20% | 4 passages (complex/abstract/idiomatic) + MCK/gapped text/proofreading | 20 min |
| Use of English | 20-24 items | 15% | Advanced structures, idioms, register, cohesive devices | 18 min |
| Speaking | 7-8 tasks | 25% | Complex argumentation, speculation, nuanced expression | 15 min |
| Writing | 2 tasks | 15% | Essay (250-300 words) + formal academic/professional writing | 15 min |

### 2.2 Key Design Principles

1. **Progressive Difficulty**: Setiap level membangun dari level sebelumnya dengan jumlah item, kompleksitas, dan waktu yang bertambah
2. **Balanced Skills**: Listening dan Speaking mendapat bobot lebih tinggi karena fokus produk pada conversation
3. **Realistic Timing**: Waktu per section dirancang untuk memungkinkan pemikiran yang cukup tanpa membuat test terlalu lama
4. **Consistent Format**: Struktur exam sama di semua level untuk memudahkan familiarisasi, dengan kompleksitas yang meningkat
