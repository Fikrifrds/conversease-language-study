# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** I don't feel well today.  
**Leo:** Oh no. What's wrong?  
**Mina:** I have a headache and I'm really tired.  
**Leo:** Did you sleep late last night?  
**Mina:** Yes. I studied and went to bed late.  
**Leo:** What did you do yesterday?  
**Mina:** I went to the clinic and then I stayed home.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Leo
