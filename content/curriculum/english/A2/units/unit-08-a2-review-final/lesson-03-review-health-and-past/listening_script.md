# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** (sighs) I don't feel well today.
**Hilmi:** Oh no. What's wrong?
**Faris:** I have a headache and I'm really tired.
**Hilmi:** Did you sleep late last night?
**Faris:** Yes. I studied and went to bed late.
**Hilmi:** And what did you do yesterday?
**Faris:** I went to the clinic and then I stayed home.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
