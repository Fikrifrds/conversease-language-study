# Transcript Translation

- **Faris:** I don't feel well today. -> Aku nggak enak badan hari ini.
- **Hilmi:** Oh no. What's wrong? -> Aduh. Kenapa?
- **Faris:** I have a headache and I'm really tired. -> Aku sakit kepala dan capek banget.
- **Hilmi:** Did you sleep late last night? -> Kamu tidur larut tadi malam?
- **Faris:** Yes. I studied and went to bed late. -> Iya. Aku belajar dan tidur larut.
- **Hilmi:** And what did you do yesterday? -> Terus, kamu ngapain kemarin?
- **Faris:** I went to the clinic and then I stayed home. -> Aku pergi ke klinik lalu di rumah aja.
