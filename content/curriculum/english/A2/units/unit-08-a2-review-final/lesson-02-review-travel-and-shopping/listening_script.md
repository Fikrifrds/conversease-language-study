# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** Excuse me. Where is platform 2?  
**Staff:** It's over there, next to the sign.  
**Mina:** Thanks. What time is the next train?  
**Staff:** At 4:15.  
**Mina:** Great. Also, do you have a phone charger?  
**Staff:** Yes. What kind do you need?  
**Mina:** A USB-C charger, please.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Staff and Mina
