# Dialogue Script

**Raka:** Excuse me. Where is platform 2?
**Staff:** It's over there, next to the sign.
**Raka:** Thanks. What time is the next train?
**Staff:** At 4:15.
**Raka:** Great. Also, do you have a phone charger?
**Staff:** Yes. What kind do you need?
**Raka:** A USB-C charger, please.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Staff and Raka
