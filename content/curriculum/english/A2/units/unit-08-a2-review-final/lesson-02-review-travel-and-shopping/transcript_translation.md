# Transcript Translation

- **Mina:** Excuse me. Where is platform 2? -> Permisi. Peron 2 di mana?
- **Staff:** It's over there, next to the sign. -> Di sana, dekat papan tanda.
- **Mina:** Thanks. What time is the next train? -> Makasih. Kereta berikutnya jam berapa?
- **Staff:** At 4:15. -> Jam 4:15.
- **Mina:** Great. Also, do you have a phone charger? -> Oke. Terus, ada charger HP?
- **Staff:** Yes. What kind do you need? -> Ada. Jenis yang mana?
- **Mina:** A USB-C charger, please. -> Charger USB-C, ya.
