# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hey! Long time no see. How have you been?
**Hilmi:** Pretty good. Busy with work.
**Faris:** Oh yeah? What have you been working on?
**Hilmi:** A new project. It's stressful, but fun.
**Faris:** Do you want to grab coffee this weekend?
**Hilmi:** Sure. Are you free on Saturday afternoon?
**Faris:** Yes. Let's meet at 3 p.m.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
