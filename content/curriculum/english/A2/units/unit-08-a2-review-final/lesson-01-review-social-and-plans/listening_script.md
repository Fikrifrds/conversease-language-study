# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** Hey! Long time no see. How have you been?  
**Leo:** Pretty good. Busy with work.  
**Mina:** Oh yeah? What have you been working on?  
**Leo:** A new project. It's stressful, but fun.  
**Mina:** Do you want to grab coffee this weekend?  
**Leo:** Sure. Are you free on Saturday afternoon?  
**Mina:** Yes. Let's meet at 3 p.m.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Leo
