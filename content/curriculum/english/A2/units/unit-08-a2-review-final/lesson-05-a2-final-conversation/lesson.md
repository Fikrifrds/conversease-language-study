# A2 Final Conversation

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can complete a longer everyday conversation using multiple A2 skills (plans, past, opinions, requests).

## Situation

Final: kamu ketemu teman. Kamu small talk, cerita kemarin, kasih opini, lalu bikin rencana dan minta bantuan kecil.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
