# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** Hey Hilmi! How have you been?  
**Hilmi:** Good. (sighs) A bit tired, though.  
**Mina:** Oh no. Did you sleep late last night?  
**Hilmi:** Yes. I worked on a project.  
**Mina:** I went to a new cafe yesterday. It was great.  
**Hilmi:** Really? Would you recommend it?  
**Mina:** Yes, because the coffee is good and it is quiet.  
**Hilmi:** Nice. Do you want to go there this weekend?  
**Mina:** Sure. Are you free on Saturday afternoon?  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Hilmi
