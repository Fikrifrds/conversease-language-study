# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hey Hilmi! How have you been?
**Hilmi:** Good. (sighs) A bit tired, though.
**Faris:** Oh no. Did you sleep late last night?
**Hilmi:** Yes. I worked on a project.
**Faris:** I went to a new cafe yesterday. It was great.
**Hilmi:** Really? Would you recommend it?
**Faris:** Yes, because the coffee is good and it is quiet.
**Hilmi:** Nice. Do you want to go there this weekend?
**Faris:** Sure. Are you free on Saturday afternoon?
**Hilmi:** Yes, I'm free on Saturday afternoon.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
