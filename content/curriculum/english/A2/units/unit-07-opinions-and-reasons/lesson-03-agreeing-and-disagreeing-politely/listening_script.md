# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** I think this restaurant is the best.
**Faris:** I'm not sure. It's a bit expensive.
**Hilmi:** Really? I think the food is worth it.
**Faris:** I agree the food is good, but the service is slow.
**Hilmi:** Oh, I didn't notice.
**Faris:** Maybe we can try another place next time.
**Hilmi:** Yeah, that's fair.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
