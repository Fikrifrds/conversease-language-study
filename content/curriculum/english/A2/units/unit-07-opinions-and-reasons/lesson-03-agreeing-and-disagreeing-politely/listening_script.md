# Dialogue Script

**Leo:** I think this restaurant is the best.  
**Mina:** I'm not sure. It's a bit expensive.  
**Leo:** Really? I think the food is worth it.  
**Mina:** I agree the food is good, but the service is slow.  
**Leo:** Oh, I didn't notice.  
**Mina:** Maybe we can try another place next time.  
**Leo:** Yeah, that's fair.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Leo
