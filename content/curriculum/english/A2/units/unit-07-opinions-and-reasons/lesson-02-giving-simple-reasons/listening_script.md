# Dialogue Script

**Leo:** Do you like this cafe?  
**Mina:** Yes, I like it.  
**Leo:** Why?  
**Mina:** Because the coffee is good.  
**Leo:** I agree. And it is quiet.  
**Mina:** Yeah. I come here often because it is relaxing.  
**Leo:** Nice choice.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Leo
