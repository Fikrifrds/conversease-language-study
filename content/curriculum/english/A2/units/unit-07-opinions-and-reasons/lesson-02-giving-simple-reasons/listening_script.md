# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** Do you like this cafe?
**Faris:** Yes, I like it.
**Hilmi:** Why?
**Faris:** Because the coffee is good.
**Hilmi:** I agree. And it is quiet.
**Faris:** Yeah. I come here often because it is relaxing.
**Hilmi:** Nice choice.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
