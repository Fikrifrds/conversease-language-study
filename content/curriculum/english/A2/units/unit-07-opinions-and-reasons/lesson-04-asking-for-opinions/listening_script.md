# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** What do you think about going to Bali?
**Hilmi:** I think it's a great idea.
**Faris:** Why?
**Hilmi:** Because the beaches are beautiful.
**Faris:** I agree. But it might be crowded.
**Hilmi:** True. Maybe we go on a weekday.
**Faris:** Good plan.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
