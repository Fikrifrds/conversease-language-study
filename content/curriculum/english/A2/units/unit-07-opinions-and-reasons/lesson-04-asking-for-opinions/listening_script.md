# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** What do you think about going to Bali?  
**Hilmi:** I think it's a great idea.  
**Mina:** Why?  
**Hilmi:** Because the beaches are beautiful.  
**Mina:** I agree. But it might be crowded.  
**Hilmi:** True. Maybe we go on a weekday.  
**Mina:** Good plan.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Hilmi
