# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** I watched the new movie last night.
**Hilmi:** Oh yeah? What did you think?
**Faris:** I think it's really good.
**Hilmi:** Nice. I want to watch it too.
**Faris:** The story is simple, but fun.
**Hilmi:** Cool. Who is in it?
**Faris:** A famous actor. He was great.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
