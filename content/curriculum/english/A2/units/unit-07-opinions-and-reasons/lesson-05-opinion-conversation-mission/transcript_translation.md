# Transcript Translation

- **Mina:** What do you think about this restaurant? -> Menurut kamu gimana restoran ini?
- **Friend:** I think it's good because it's cheap. -> Menurutku oke karena murah.
- **Mina:** I'm not sure. The service might be slow. -> Aku kurang yakin. Pelayanannya mungkin lambat.
- **Friend:** That's fair. Do you have another idea? -> Masuk akal. Kamu ada ide lain?
- **Mina:** Yes. I think the cafe next door is better. -> Iya. Menurutku kafe sebelah lebih bagus.
- **Friend:** Why? -> Kenapa?
- **Mina:** Because it's quieter and the coffee is good. -> Karena lebih tenang dan kopinya enak.
