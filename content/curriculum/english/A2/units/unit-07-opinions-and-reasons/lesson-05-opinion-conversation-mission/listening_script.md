# Dialogue Script

**Raka:** What do you think about this restaurant?
**Friend:** I think it's good because it's cheap.
**Raka:** I'm not sure. The service might be slow.
**Friend:** That's fair. Do you have another idea?
**Raka:** Yes. I think the cafe next door is better.
**Friend:** Why?
**Raka:** Because it's quieter and the coffee is good.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Raka and Friend
