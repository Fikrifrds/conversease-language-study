# Transcript Translation

- **Friend:** Hey! What did you do yesterday? -> Hai! Kamu ngapain kemarin?
- **Mina:** I went to the museum and took photos. -> Aku pergi ke museum dan foto-foto.
- **Friend:** Nice. How was it? -> Asik. Gimana?
- **Mina:** It was really interesting. I liked it. -> Seru banget. Aku suka.
- **Friend:** Did you go with anyone? -> Kamu pergi sama siapa?
- **Mina:** Yes, I went with my friend. -> Iya, aku pergi sama temanku.
- **Friend:** Sounds fun. -> Kedengarannya seru.
