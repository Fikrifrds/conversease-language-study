# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** Hey, how was your day yesterday?  
**Hilmi:** It was good. I went to the mall.  
**Mina:** Nice. What did you do there?  
**Hilmi:** I bought a jacket and ate ramen.  
**Mina:** Sounds great. Did you go alone?  
**Hilmi:** No, I went with my brother.  
**Mina:** Cool. I watched a movie last night.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Hilmi
