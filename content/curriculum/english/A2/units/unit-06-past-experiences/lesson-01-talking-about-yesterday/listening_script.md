# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hey, how was your day yesterday?
**Hilmi:** It was good. I went to the mall.
**Faris:** Nice. What did you do there?
**Hilmi:** I bought a jacket and ate ramen.
**Faris:** Sounds great. Did you go alone?
**Hilmi:** No, I went with my brother.
**Faris:** Cool. I watched a movie last night.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
