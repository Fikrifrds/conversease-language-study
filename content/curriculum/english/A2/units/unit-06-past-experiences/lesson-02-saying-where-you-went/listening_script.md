# Dialogue Script

**Staff:** Hi! Did you go anywhere interesting yesterday?
**Raka:** Yes, I went to the museum.
**Staff:** Oh nice. Where did you go after that?
**Raka:** I went to a cafe near the river.
**Staff:** Sounds relaxing.
**Raka:** Yeah. Where did you go?
**Staff:** I stayed home.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Raka and Staff
