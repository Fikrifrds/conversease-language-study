# Dialogue Script

**Staff:** Hi! Did you go anywhere interesting yesterday?  
**Mina:** Yes, I went to the museum.  
**Staff:** Oh nice. Where did you go after that?  
**Mina:** I went to a cafe near the river.  
**Staff:** Sounds relaxing.  
**Mina:** Yeah. Where did you go?  
**Staff:** I stayed home.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Staff
