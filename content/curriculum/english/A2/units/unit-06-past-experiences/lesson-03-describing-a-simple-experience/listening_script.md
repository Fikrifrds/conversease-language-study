# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** I tried a new restaurant yesterday.
**Hilmi:** Oh nice. How was it?
**Faris:** It was delicious. I really liked it.
**Hilmi:** What did you eat?
**Faris:** I ate grilled chicken and salad.
**Hilmi:** Sounds healthy.
**Faris:** Yeah, and the service was great.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
