# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** What did you do yesterday evening?
**Hilmi:** I cooked dinner and listened to music.
**Faris:** Nice. Did you cook at home?
**Hilmi:** Yes, I did.
**Faris:** What did you cook?
**Hilmi:** I made fried rice.
**Faris:** Sounds good.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
