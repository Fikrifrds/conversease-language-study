# Transcript Translation

- **Mina:** What did you do yesterday evening? -> Kamu ngapain tadi malam?
- **Leo:** I cooked dinner and listened to music. -> Aku masak makan malam dan dengerin musik.
- **Mina:** Nice. Did you cook at home? -> Asik. Kamu masak di rumah?
- **Leo:** Yes, I did. -> Iya.
- **Mina:** What did you cook? -> Kamu masak apa?
- **Leo:** I made fried rice. -> Aku bikin nasi goreng.
- **Mina:** Sounds good. -> Kedengarannya enak.
