# Dialogue Script

**Staff:** Hi. Where are you going?
**Dimas:** Hi. I'd like one ticket to Bandung, please.
**Staff:** One-way or round-trip?
**Dimas:** One-way, please.
**Staff:** Okay. It leaves at 6:30 pm from platform 2.
**Dimas:** Great. Thank you.
**Driver:** Hi. Where to?
**Dimas:** Hi. Can you take me to my hotel, please?
**Driver:** Sure. How long is your train trip?
**Dimas:** About three hours.
**Driver:** Okay. Let's go.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: clear, supportive, confident
- Voices: one staff member, one driver, one male traveler
