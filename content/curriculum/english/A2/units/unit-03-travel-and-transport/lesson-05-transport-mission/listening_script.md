# Dialogue Script

**Staff:** Hi. Where are you going?  
**Alya:** Hi. I'd like one ticket to Bandung, please.  
**Staff:** One-way or round-trip?  
**Alya:** One-way, please.  
**Staff:** Okay. It leaves at 6:30 pm from platform 2.  
**Alya:** Great. Thank you.  
**Driver:** Hi. Where to?  
**Alya:** Hi. Can you take me to my hotel, please?  
**Driver:** Sure. How long is your train trip?  
**Alya:** About three hours.  
**Driver:** Okay. Let's go.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: clear, supportive, confident
- Voices: one staff member, one driver, one female traveler
