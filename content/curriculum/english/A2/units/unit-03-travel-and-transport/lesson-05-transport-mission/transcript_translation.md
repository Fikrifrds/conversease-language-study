# Transcript Translation

- **Staff:** Hi. Where are you going? -> Hai. Kamu mau ke mana?
- **Dimas:** Hi. I'd like one ticket to Bandung, please. -> Hai. Saya mau satu tiket ke Bandung, ya.
- **Staff:** One-way or round-trip? -> Sekali jalan atau pulang-pergi?
- **Dimas:** One-way, please. -> Sekali jalan, ya.
- **Staff:** Okay. It leaves at 6:30 pm from platform 2. -> Oke. Berangkat jam 6.30 sore dari platform 2.
- **Dimas:** Great. Thank you. -> Oke. Terima kasih.
- **Driver:** Hi. Where to? -> Hai. Mau ke mana?
- **Dimas:** Hi. Can you take me to my hotel, please? -> Hai. Bisa antar saya ke hotel saya, ya?
- **Driver:** Sure. How long is your train trip? -> Oke. Perjalanan keretamu berapa lama?
- **Dimas:** About three hours. -> Sekitar tiga jam.
- **Driver:** Okay. Let's go. -> Oke. Ayo berangkat.
