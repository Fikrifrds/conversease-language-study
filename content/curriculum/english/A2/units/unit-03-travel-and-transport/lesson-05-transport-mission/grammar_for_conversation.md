# Grammar for Conversation

Use **I'd like + noun** to request something.

Examples:

- I'd like one ticket to Bandung, please.
- I'd like a taxi, please.

Use **What time does it leave?** to ask about a schedule.

Examples:

- What time does it leave?
- What time does the train leave?

Use **Can you take me to + place?** for a driver request.

Examples:

- Can you take me to my hotel?
- Can you take me to the station?
