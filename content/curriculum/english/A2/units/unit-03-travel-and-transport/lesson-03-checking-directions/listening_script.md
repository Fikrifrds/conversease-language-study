# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Excuse me. Where is platform 2?  
**Staff:** Go straight and turn left at the stairs.  
**Ben:** Turn left at the stairs. Okay.  
**Staff:** Yes. Platform 2 is on the left.  
**Ben:** Great. Is this the right way?  
**Staff:** Yes, you're going the right way.  
**Ben:** Thank you.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: clear, helpful, friendly
- Voices: one male traveler, one staff member
