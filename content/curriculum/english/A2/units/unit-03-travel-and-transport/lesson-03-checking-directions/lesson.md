# Checking Directions

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can check directions and confirm they are going the right way.

## Situation

Kamu sedang di stasiun atau terminal. Kamu mau ke gate/platform tertentu, tapi kamu ragu. Kamu minta arahan dan konfirmasi.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful direction-checking phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
