# Transcript Translation

- **Driver:** Hi. Where to? -> Hai. Mau ke mana?
- **Adi:** Hi. Can you take me to the station, please? -> Hai. Bisa antar saya ke stasiun, ya?
- **Driver:** Sure. To the station. -> Oke. Ke stasiun.
- **Adi:** Thanks. How long will it take? -> Terima kasih. Kira-kira berapa lama?
- **Driver:** About 20 minutes. -> Sekitar 20 menit.
- **Adi:** Okay. Please take the fastest route. -> Oke. Tolong lewat rute tercepat.
- **Driver:** No problem. -> Tidak apa-apa.
