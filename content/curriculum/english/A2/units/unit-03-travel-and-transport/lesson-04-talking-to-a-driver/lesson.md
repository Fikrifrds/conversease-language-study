# Talking to a Driver

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can tell a driver where they want to go and ask about the travel time.

## Situation

Kamu naik taksi/ojek online. Kamu mau ke hotel atau stasiun. Kamu menyebutkan tujuan dan tanya berapa lama.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful driver phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
