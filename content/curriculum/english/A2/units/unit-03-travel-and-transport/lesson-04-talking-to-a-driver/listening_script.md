# Dialogue Script

**Driver:** Hi. Where to?
**Adi:** Hi. Can you take me to the station, please?
**Driver:** Sure. To the station.
**Adi:** Thanks. How long will it take?
**Driver:** About 20 minutes.
**Adi:** Okay. Please take the fastest route.
**Driver:** No problem.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: one driver, one male traveler
