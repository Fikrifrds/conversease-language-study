# Dialogue Script

**Driver:** Hi. Where to?  
**Lina:** Hi. Can you take me to the station, please?  
**Driver:** Sure. To the station.  
**Lina:** Thanks. How long will it take?  
**Driver:** About 20 minutes.  
**Lina:** Okay. Please take the fastest route.  
**Driver:** No problem.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: one driver, one female traveler
