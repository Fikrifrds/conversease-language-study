# Buying a Ticket

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can buy a simple ticket and confirm the destination.

## Situation

Kamu ada di loket stasiun. Kamu ingin beli tiket ke satu kota, lalu memastikan jenis tiket dan tujuan.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful ticket phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
