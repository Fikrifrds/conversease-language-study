# Dialogue Script

**Staff:** Hi. Where are you going?  
**Dimas:** Hi. I'd like one ticket to Bandung, please.  
**Staff:** One-way or round-trip?  
**Dimas:** One-way, please.  
**Staff:** Okay. That's 120,000 rupiah.  
**Dimas:** Okay. How much is it again?  
**Staff:** 120,000 rupiah.  
**Dimas:** Great. Thank you.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: clear, helpful, friendly
- Voices: one staff member, one male traveler
