# Dialogue Script

**Alya:** Excuse me. What time does the train leave?  
**Staff:** Which train?  
**Alya:** The train to Bandung.  
**Staff:** It leaves at 6:30 pm.  
**Alya:** Okay. Which platform?  
**Staff:** Platform 2.  
**Alya:** Great. How long is the trip?  
**Staff:** About three hours.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: clear, helpful, friendly
- Voices: one female traveler, one staff member
