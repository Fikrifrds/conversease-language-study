# Dialogue Script

**Dimas:** Excuse me. What time does the train leave?
**Staff:** Which train?
**Dimas:** The train to Bandung.
**Staff:** It leaves at 6:30 pm.
**Dimas:** Okay. Which platform?
**Staff:** Platform 2.
**Dimas:** Great. How long is the trip?
**Staff:** About three hours.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: clear, helpful, friendly
- Voices: one male traveler, one staff member
