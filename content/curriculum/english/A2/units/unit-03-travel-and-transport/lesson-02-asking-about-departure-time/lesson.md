# Asking About Departure Time

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can ask about a departure time and confirm the platform.

## Situation

Kamu sudah punya tiket. Kamu ingin tahu kereta berangkat jam berapa, dari platform mana, dan berapa lama perjalanan.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful departure phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
