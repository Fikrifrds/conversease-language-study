# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** Hi Ben. Can we reschedule our coffee?  
**Ben:** Sure. What happened?  
**Mina:** Something came up at work.  
**Ben:** No problem. When are you free?  
**Mina:** How about Friday evening?  
**Ben:** Friday is good. Does 7 pm work for you?  
**Mina:** Yes, 7 pm works. Same cafe?  
**Ben:** Yes. See you on Friday.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, polite, supportive
- Voices: one female friend, one male friend
