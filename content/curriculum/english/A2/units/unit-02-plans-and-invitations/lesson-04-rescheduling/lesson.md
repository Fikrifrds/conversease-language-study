# Rescheduling

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can reschedule a plan and agree on a new time.

## Situation

Kamu sudah punya rencana, tapi tiba-tiba tidak bisa. Kamu minta reschedule dan menawarkan waktu baru.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful rescheduling phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
