# Transcript Translation

- **Ben:** Hi Mina. Do you want to grab coffee tomorrow? -> Hai Mina. Kamu mau ngopi besok?
- **Mina:** Thanks, but I can't tomorrow. -> Terima kasih, tapi saya tidak bisa besok.
- **Ben:** Oh, okay. Are you busy? -> Oh, oke. Kamu sibuk?
- **Mina:** Yes. Something came up at work. -> Iya. Ada urusan mendadak di kerjaan.
- **Ben:** No problem. How about Saturday? -> Tidak apa-apa. Gimana kalau Sabtu?
- **Mina:** Saturday is good. What time? -> Sabtu oke. Jam berapa?
- **Ben:** Does 3 pm work for you? -> Jam 3 sore cocok buat kamu?
- **Mina:** Yes, 3 pm works. Where should we meet? -> Iya, jam 3 sore cocok. Kita ketemu di mana?
- **Ben:** Let's meet at the cafe near the station. -> Yuk ketemu di kafe dekat stasiun.
- **Mina:** Great. See you then. -> Oke. Sampai ketemu nanti.
