# Accepting and Declining

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can accept or decline an invitation politely with a short reason.

## Situation

Temanmu ngajak kamu ikut acara. Kamu bisa bilang iya, atau menolak dengan sopan dan alasan singkat.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice accepting and declining phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
