# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Hey Mina. Are you free tomorrow?  
**Mina:** Tomorrow? Yes, I think so. Why?  
**Ben:** Let's get coffee after work.  
**Mina:** Sure. What time?  
**Ben:** How about 6 pm?  
**Mina:** 6 pm works for me. Where should we meet?  
**Ben:** Let's meet at the cafe near the station.  
**Mina:** Great. See you then.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one male friend, one female friend
