# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Hey Faris. Are you free tomorrow?
**Faris:** Tomorrow? Yes, I think so. Why?
**Ben:** Let's get coffee after work.
**Faris:** Sure. What time?
**Ben:** How about 6 pm?
**Faris:** 6 pm works for me. Where should we meet?
**Ben:** Let's meet at the cafe near the station.
**Faris:** Great. See you then.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one male friend, one female friend
