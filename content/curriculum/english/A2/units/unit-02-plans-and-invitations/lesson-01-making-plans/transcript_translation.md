# Transcript Translation

- **Ben:** Hey Faris. Are you free tomorrow? -> Hei Faris. Kamu kosong besok?
- **Faris:** Tomorrow? Yes, I think so. Why? -> Besok? Iya, sepertinya. Kenapa?
- **Ben:** Let's get coffee after work. -> Yuk ngopi setelah kerja.
- **Faris:** Sure. What time? -> Boleh. Jam berapa?
- **Ben:** How about 6 pm? -> Gimana kalau jam 6 sore?
- **Faris:** 6 pm works for me. Where should we meet? -> Jam 6 sore cocok buat saya. Kita ketemu di mana?
- **Ben:** Let's meet at the cafe near the station. -> Yuk ketemu di kafe dekat stasiun.
- **Faris:** Great. See you then. -> Oke. Sampai ketemu nanti.
