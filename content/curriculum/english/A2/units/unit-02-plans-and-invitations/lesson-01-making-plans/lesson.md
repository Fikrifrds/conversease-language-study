# Making Plans

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can make a simple plan with a time and a place.

## Situation

Kamu ngobrol dengan teman setelah kelas. Kamu mau ngajak ketemu sebentar, pilih waktu, dan pilih tempat.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful planning phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
