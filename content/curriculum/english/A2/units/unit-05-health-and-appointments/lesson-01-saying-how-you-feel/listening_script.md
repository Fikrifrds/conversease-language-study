# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hey, are you okay?
**Hilmi:** Not really. I don't feel well.
**Faris:** What's wrong?
**Hilmi:** I have a headache, and I'm really tired.
**Faris:** Do you want to go home?
**Hilmi:** Yes. I think I have a fever.
**Faris:** Okay. Take care.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Hilmi, Faris
