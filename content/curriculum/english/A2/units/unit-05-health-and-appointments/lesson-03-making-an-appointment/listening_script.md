# Dialogue Script

**Receptionist:** Good morning. Green Clinic. How can I help you?
**Raka:** Hi. I'd like to make an appointment.
**Receptionist:** Sure. What day works for you?
**Raka:** Is tomorrow afternoon okay?
**Receptionist:** We have 3:30 p.m. available.
**Raka:** Great. I'll take it.
**Receptionist:** Can I have your name, please?
**Raka:** Yes, it's Raka.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Raka, Receptionist
