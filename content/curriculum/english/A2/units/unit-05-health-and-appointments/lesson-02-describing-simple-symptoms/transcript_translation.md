# Transcript Translation

- **Nurse:** What seems to be the problem? -> Keluhannya apa?
- **Mina:** I've had a sore throat since yesterday. -> Saya sakit tenggorokan sejak kemarin.
- **Nurse:** Do you have a fever? -> Kamu demam?
- **Mina:** A little. And I have a cough. -> Sedikit. Dan saya batuk.
- **Nurse:** How long have you been coughing? -> Sudah berapa lama kamu batuk?
- **Mina:** For two days. -> Dua hari.
- **Nurse:** Okay. Please wait here. -> Oke. Tolong tunggu di sini.
