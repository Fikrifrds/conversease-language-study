# Dialogue Script

**Nurse:** What seems to be the problem?  
**Mina:** I've had a sore throat since yesterday.  
**Nurse:** Do you have a fever?  
**Mina:** A little. And I have a cough.  
**Nurse:** How long have you been coughing?  
**Mina:** For two days.  
**Nurse:** Okay. Please wait here.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina, Nurse
