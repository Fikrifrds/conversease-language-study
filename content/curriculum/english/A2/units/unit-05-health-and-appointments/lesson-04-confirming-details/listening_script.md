# Dialogue Script

**Receptionist:** Okay, you're booked for tomorrow at 3:30 p.m.
**Raka:** Great. Let me confirm the time: 3:30 p.m., right?
**Receptionist:** Yes, that's right.
**Raka:** And my name is Raka Park. P-A-R-K.
**Receptionist:** Thank you. Do you have a phone number?
**Raka:** Yes. It's 0812-345-678.
**Receptionist:** Perfect. See you tomorrow.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Raka, Receptionist
