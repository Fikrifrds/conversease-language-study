# Grammar for Conversation

Use **I'm looking for + item** to explain what you want.

Examples:

- I'm looking for a charger.
- I'm looking for a T-shirt.

Use **Which one is + comparative?** to compare.

Examples:

- Which one is cheaper?
- Which one is better?

Use **Could you + verb?** to ask politely.

Examples:

- Could you help me?
- Could you show me where it is?
