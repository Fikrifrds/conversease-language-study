# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Excuse me. I'm looking for a charger.  
**Staff:** Sure. What kind?  
**Ben:** A USB-C charger. Do you have it?  
**Staff:** Yes. We have this one and that one.  
**Ben:** Which one is cheaper?  
**Staff:** This one is cheaper.  
**Ben:** Okay. I'll take this one.  
**Staff:** Great choice. Anything else?  
**Ben:** Also, could you help me? Where are the batteries?  
**Staff:** They're over there, next to the cash register.  
**Ben:** Great. Thank you.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one male customer, one staff member
