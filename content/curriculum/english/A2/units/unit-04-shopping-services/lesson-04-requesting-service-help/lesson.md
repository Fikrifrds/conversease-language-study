# Requesting Service Help

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can ask for simple service help and respond politely.

## Situation

Kamu butuh bantuan di toko. Kamu minta staff menunjukkan lokasi barang, atau membantu cari ukuran.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful service phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
