# Dialogue Script

**Lina:** Excuse me. Could you help me?  
**Staff:** Sure. What do you need?  
**Lina:** Can you show me where the batteries are?  
**Staff:** Yes. They're over there, next to the cash register.  
**Lina:** Great. Thank you for your help.  
**Staff:** You're welcome.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: one female customer, one staff member
