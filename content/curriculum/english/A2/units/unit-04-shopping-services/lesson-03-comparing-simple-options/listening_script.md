# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Excuse me. Which one is cheaper?  
**Staff:** This one is cheaper. It's 80,000 rupiah.  
**Ben:** And the other one?  
**Staff:** The other one is 120,000 rupiah, but it's better quality.  
**Ben:** Okay. I'll take the cheaper one.  
**Staff:** Sure.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: one male customer, one staff member
