# Transcript Translation

- **Dimas:** Excuse me. Do you have this T-shirt in black? -> Permisi. Ada kaus ini warna hitam?
- **Staff:** Sorry, we don't have black. We have blue and white. -> Maaf, tidak ada hitam. Ada biru dan putih.
- **Dimas:** Okay. Do you have it in size M? -> Oke. Ada ukuran M?
- **Staff:** Yes, size M is available. -> Ada, ukuran M tersedia.
- **Dimas:** Great. I'll take the blue one. -> Oke. Saya ambil yang biru.
- **Staff:** Sure. -> Oke.
