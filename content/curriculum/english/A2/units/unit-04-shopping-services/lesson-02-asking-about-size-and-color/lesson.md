# Asking About Size and Color

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can ask about size and color and choose one option.

## Situation

Kamu beli baju di toko. Kamu tanya warna dan ukuran yang tersedia, lalu pilih salah satu.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful size and color phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
