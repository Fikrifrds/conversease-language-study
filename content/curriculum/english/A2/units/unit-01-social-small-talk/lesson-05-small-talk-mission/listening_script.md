# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Hi Raka. How's it going?
**Raka:** Hi Ben. I'm good, thanks. How about you?
**Ben:** (sighs) I'm a bit tired today.
**Raka:** Oh no. Did you sleep well?
**Ben:** Not really. I stayed up late.
**Raka:** I'm sorry to hear that.
**Ben:** It's okay. By the way, any plans for the weekend?
**Raka:** Yes. I'm going to a new cafe. Do you want to join?
**Ben:** (chuckle) That sounds fun. What time?
**Raka:** Saturday at 3 pm.
**Ben:** Great. See you then!
**Raka:** See you!

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, supportive, confident
- Voices: one male friend, one female friend
