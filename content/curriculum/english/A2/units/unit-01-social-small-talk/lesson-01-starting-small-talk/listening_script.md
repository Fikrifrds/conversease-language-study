# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Hi Faris. How's your day?
**Faris:** Hi Ben. It's good, thanks. How about you?
**Ben:** Pretty good. It's busy today.
**Faris:** Yeah, the office is really busy.
**Ben:** By the way, how was your weekend?
**Faris:** It was nice. I went to the park.
**Ben:** Sounds great. Have a good day!
**Faris:** You too. See you later.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one male colleague, one female colleague
