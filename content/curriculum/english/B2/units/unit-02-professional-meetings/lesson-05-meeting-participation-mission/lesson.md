# Meeting Participation Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can complete a mini meeting: open a point, clarify scope, give constructive feedback, and summarize decisions with action items.

## Situation

Misi: kamu aktif di meeting secara profesional dari awal sampai rangkuman akhir.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
