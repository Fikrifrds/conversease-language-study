# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** We want to improve our onboarding experience.
**Faris:** Got it. Just to clarify, who is the main user group?
**Ilham:** Mostly new hires in engineering.
**Faris:** Thanks. Could you share more about the biggest pain point today?
**Ilham:** They don't know where to find key docs.
**Faris:** Understood. So what you need is a clear starting point and a simple checklist, right?
**Ilham:** Exactly.
**Faris:** Great. I'll summarize the requirements and share a proposal.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, empathetic, clear
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
