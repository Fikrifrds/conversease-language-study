# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** I'm concerned this change will disrupt our team.
**Faris:** I understand the concern. Could you clarify what disruption you expect?
**Ilham:** People might miss updates.
**Faris:** That makes sense. To reduce the risk, we can send a weekly summary and keep a monthly live meeting.
**Ilham:** Okay, that helps.
**Faris:** We can also run a two-week trial and gather feedback.
**Ilham:** Sounds reasonable.
**Faris:** Great. I'll share a draft plan today.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, empathetic, clear
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
