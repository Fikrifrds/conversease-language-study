# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** What are our options?
**Faris:** We have two options. Option A is a quick fix we can deliver this week.
**Ilham:** And option B?
**Faris:** Option B is a more robust solution, but it takes two more weeks.
**Ilham:** What's the trade-off?
**Faris:** The trade-off is speed versus long-term stability.
**Ilham:** What do you recommend?
**Faris:** I'd recommend option B if the timeline allows.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, empathetic, clear
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
