# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** We want to improve onboarding. What can you do?
**Faris:** Just to clarify, who is the main user group and what's the biggest pain point?
**Ilham:** New hires can't find key docs.
**Faris:** Got it. We have two options: a quick fix this week, or a more robust solution in two weeks.
**Ilham:** I'm concerned it will disrupt our team.
**Faris:** I understand the concern. To reduce the risk, we can run a two-week pilot and send weekly summaries.
**Ilham:** Okay. What happens next?
**Faris:** To confirm, next steps are: I'll send a draft plan today, and you'll review it by Friday. Does that timeline work for you?
**Ilham:** Yes, that works for me.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, empathetic, clear
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
