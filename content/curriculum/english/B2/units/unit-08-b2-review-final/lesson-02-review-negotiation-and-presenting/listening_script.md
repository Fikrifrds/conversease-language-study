# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** We need this delivered next week.
**Faris:** Here's my proposal: we deliver a smaller scope next week, then the full version two weeks later.
**Ilham:** What's the trade-off?
**Faris:** The trade-off is speed versus completeness.
**Ilham:** Can we meet in the middle?
**Faris:** Yes. On the other hand, we can include the top two features now and postpone the rest.
**Ilham:** So what do you recommend?
**Faris:** I'd recommend that compromise. To summarize: top two features next week, the rest in two weeks.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: confident, professional, friendly
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
