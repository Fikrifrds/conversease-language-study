# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** We saw a report saying complaints are up. Is it reliable?
**Faris:** Based on the source, it's a partial snapshot. We should verify it with our support data.
**Ilham:** Clients are worried about delays.
**Faris:** I understand the concern. Just to clarify, what timeline did we promise?
**Ilham:** Two weeks.
**Faris:** Got it. Next steps are: I'll share an update today, and we'll confirm a revised timeline by Friday.
**Ilham:** Sounds good.
**Faris:** Great. We'll keep you posted with weekly summaries.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: confident, professional, friendly
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
