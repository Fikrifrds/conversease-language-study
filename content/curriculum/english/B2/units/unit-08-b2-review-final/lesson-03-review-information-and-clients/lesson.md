# Review Information and Clients

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can discuss information sources carefully and handle a client call with clarity, empathy, and next steps.

## Situation

Review: kamu bahas sumber informasi (reliability) dan jalankan mini client call: clarify, handle concerns, confirm next steps.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
