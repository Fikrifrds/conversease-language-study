# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** We need to decide our approach for the next quarter.
**Faris:** Great. Let's define the scope and what success looks like.
**Ilham:** We want fewer complaints and faster completion.
**Faris:** Based on the data, the billing redesign increased drop-offs, so we should address that first.
**Ilham:** But leadership wants new features too.
**Faris:** I understand the concern. The trade-off is speed versus reliability. Given these constraints, I'd recommend a time-boxed pilot.
**Ilham:** Okay. What are the next steps?
**Faris:** Next steps are: I'll share a plan today, and you'll review it by Friday. Then we finalize on Monday.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: confident, professional, friendly
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
