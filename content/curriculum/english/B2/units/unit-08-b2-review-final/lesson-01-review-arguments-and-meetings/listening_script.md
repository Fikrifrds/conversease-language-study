# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Can you summarize your position for the meeting?
**Dimas:** Sure. My position is that we should prioritize fixing the billing flow.
**Faris:** What's your main reason?
**Dimas:** The main reason is it affects revenue and support workload.
**Faris:** Any counterpoints?
**Dimas:** Some teams prefer new features, but the risk is higher if billing stays broken.
**Faris:** Okay. How do we close this?
**Dimas:** To summarize, we focus on billing first. Next steps are: I'll share a plan today, and you'll review it by Friday.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: confident, professional, friendly
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
