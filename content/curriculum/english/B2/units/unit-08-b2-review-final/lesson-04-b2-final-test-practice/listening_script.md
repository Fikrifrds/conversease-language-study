# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Final practice: what's your position?
**Dimas:** My position is that we should run a two-week pilot before a full rollout.
**Faris:** What's your evidence?
**Dimas:** Based on the data, drop-offs increased after the redesign.
**Faris:** Explain the trade-off.
**Dimas:** The trade-off is speed versus long-term reliability.
**Faris:** Make a recommendation and next steps.
**Dimas:** I'd recommend the pilot. Next steps are: I'll draft the plan today, and you'll review it by Friday.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: confident, professional, friendly
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
