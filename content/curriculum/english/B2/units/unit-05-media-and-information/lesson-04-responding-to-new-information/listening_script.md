# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** I found a report that contradicts the article we read.  
**Mina:** Oh, I wasn't aware of that.  
**Ilham:** It says the data sample was very small.  
**Mina:** That changes things. I'd like to understand the methodology.  
**Ilham:** We should check the full report.  
**Mina:** Agreed. Can you share the link?  
**Ilham:** Sure, I'll send it after this.  
**Mina:** Thanks. Then we can revise our conclusion.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, thoughtful, clear
- Voices: Mina and Ilham
