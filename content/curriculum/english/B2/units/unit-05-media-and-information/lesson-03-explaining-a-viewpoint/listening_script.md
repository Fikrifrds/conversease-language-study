# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** I read two completely opposite opinions about this today. What's your take on regulating AI tools?  
**Mina:** From my perspective, some regulation is necessary. Especially for tools that reach millions of people.  
**Faris:** Why do you say that? A lot of people argue the market will correct itself.  
**Mina:** The reason is it protects users from misuse and misinformation. Ordinary users can't always tell what's reliable.  
**Faris:** But regulation could slow innovation. Smaller companies might not afford the compliance costs.  
**Mina:** I see the other side, but basic safety rules can still support innovation. Clear rules actually give builders more confidence to invest.  
**Faris:** That's fair. I hadn't thought about the confidence angle before.  
**Mina:** We need balance. Strong enough to protect people, light enough to let new ideas grow.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, thoughtful, clear
- Voices: Mina and Faris
