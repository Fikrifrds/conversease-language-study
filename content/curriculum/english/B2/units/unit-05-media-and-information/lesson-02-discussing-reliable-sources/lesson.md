# Discussing Reliable Sources

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can discuss whether a source is reliable, explain why, and suggest a better source.

## Situation

Kamu bahas berita. Kamu nilai sumbernya reliable atau tidak, jelasin alasannya, lalu saranin sumber yang lebih baik.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
