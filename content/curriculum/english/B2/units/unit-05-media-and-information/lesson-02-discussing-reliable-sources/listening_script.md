# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** I saw a claim on social media. Is it true?
**Faris:** I'm not sure that source is reliable.
**Ilham:** Why not?
**Faris:** Because it doesn't cite data or mention who wrote it.
**Ilham:** So what would you check?
**Faris:** I'd check official reports or reputable news outlets.
**Ilham:** How do you decide what's reputable?
**Faris:** I trust sources that show evidence and correct mistakes publicly.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, thoughtful, clear
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
