# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** I want to launch next week, but you want more testing.
**Dimas:** Maybe we can find a compromise.
**Faris:** Like what?
**Dimas:** A compromise could be launching to 10% of users first.
**Faris:** That could work. What about testing?
**Dimas:** If we do a small rollout, can we agree on one extra day for testing?
**Faris:** Yes, that feels fair.
**Dimas:** Great. I'll update the rollout plan.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, calm, collaborative
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
