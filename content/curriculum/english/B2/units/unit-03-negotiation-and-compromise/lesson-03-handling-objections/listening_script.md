# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** I'm concerned this plan will slow us down.
**Faris:** I understand the concern. What part feels risky to you?
**Ilham:** The extra review steps.
**Faris:** Would it help if we limit reviews to high-risk changes only?
**Ilham:** Maybe, but who decides what's high-risk?
**Faris:** Good question. What if we define simple criteria and review them together?
**Ilham:** That sounds reasonable.
**Faris:** Great. Let's draft criteria today.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, calm, collaborative
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
