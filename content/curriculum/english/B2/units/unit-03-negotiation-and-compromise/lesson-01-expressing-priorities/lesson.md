# Expressing Priorities

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can state your priorities clearly, explain what matters most, and ask about the other person's priorities.

## Situation

Kamu lagi diskusi rencana kerja. Kamu jelasin prioritas utama kamu, jelasin alasannya singkat, lalu tanya prioritas lawan bicara.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
