# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** We have limited time. What should we focus on first?
**Dimas:** My top priority is shipping the core feature safely.
**Faris:** Why that first?
**Dimas:** Because it's the main value for users, and the deadline is close.
**Faris:** Okay. Anything else?
**Dimas:** The second priority is improving monitoring, so we can catch issues early.
**Faris:** Makes sense. What's your priority?
**Dimas:** What about you? What's your top priority?
**Faris:** My top priority is keeping scope small so we can hit the deadline.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, calm, collaborative
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
