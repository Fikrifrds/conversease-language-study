# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** We need to decide our launch plan.
**Dimas:** My top priority is quality. What matters most to you?
**Faris:** My top priority is speed.
**Dimas:** I understand. I propose we run a small pilot for two weeks.
**Faris:** I'm concerned it will delay the launch.
**Dimas:** I understand the concern. Would it help if we prepare in parallel?
**Faris:** Maybe. What's the compromise?
**Dimas:** A compromise could be launching to 10% first, then expanding if results look good.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, calm, collaborative
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
