# Negotiation Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can complete a negotiation: align priorities, make a proposal, handle objections, find middle ground, and agree on next steps.

## Situation

Misi: kamu negosiasi rencana launch. Kamu align prioritas, usul proposal, handle keberatan, cari kompromi, lalu sepakat next step.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
