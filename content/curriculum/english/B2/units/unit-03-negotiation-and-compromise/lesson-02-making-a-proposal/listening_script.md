# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** How should we handle the launch plan? Marketing keeps asking me for a firm date.
**Dimas:** I propose we do a small pilot first. It lowers the risk without blocking the bigger plan.
**Faris:** What timeline are you thinking? We can't keep the date open forever.
**Dimas:** How about we run it for two weeks and review the results? Two weeks is enough to see real usage patterns.
**Faris:** Would that delay the full launch? That's my main worry here.
**Dimas:** Not necessarily. We can prepare in parallel. The launch checklist doesn't depend on the pilot results.
**Faris:** Okay, let's draft it that way then. Would that work for you?
**Dimas:** Yes, that works for me. I'll share the pilot plan tomorrow morning.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, calm, collaborative
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
