# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** I like your idea, but I'm worried it will increase costs.
**Dimas:** That's a fair point. However, the long-term savings could be significant.
**Faris:** Can you explain why?
**Dimas:** If we reduce rework, we save engineering time and customer support time.
**Faris:** But the upfront investment is still high.
**Dimas:** I understand the concern, but we can phase it in and measure results after one month.
**Faris:** Okay, that feels more manageable.
**Dimas:** Great. Let's define success metrics first.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, clear, confident
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
