# Responding to Counterpoints

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can respond to a counterpoint politely, show you understand it, and reinforce your position.

## Situation

Kamu debat sehat. Lawan bicara kasih counterpoint, kamu respon sopan dan tetap mempertahankan argumenmu.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
