# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Why do you think onboarding should be more structured?
**Faris:** Because new hires need clarity. For example, last quarter we had three new joiners who felt lost in week one.
**Ilham:** What happened exactly?
**Faris:** They didn't know which tools to use, so they kept asking in multiple channels.
**Ilham:** Okay, that's a good point.
**Faris:** For instance, a simple checklist would reduce repeated questions.
**Ilham:** So your suggestion is a checklist plus a buddy system?
**Faris:** Yes, such as pairing each new hire with one mentor for two weeks.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, clear, confident
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
