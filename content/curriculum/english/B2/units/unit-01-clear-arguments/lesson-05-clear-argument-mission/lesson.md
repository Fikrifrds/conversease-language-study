# Clear Argument Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can complete a mini argument: state your position, give reasons and an example, respond to a counterpoint, and agree on next steps.

## Situation

Misi: kamu usul perubahan proses kerja. Kamu jelasin posisi, dua alasan, satu contoh, respon counterpoint, lalu sepakat next step.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
