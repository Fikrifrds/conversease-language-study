# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Should we switch our weekly meeting to a written update?
**Dimas:** In my view, a written update would be better for routine topics.
**Faris:** Why do you think that?
**Dimas:** Because it saves time and lets people focus on deep work.
**Faris:** But some people like real-time discussion.
**Dimas:** I see your point, but we can keep one live meeting per month for open discussions.
**Faris:** That sounds like a good compromise.
**Dimas:** Great. I'm happy to draft a proposal.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, clear, confident
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
