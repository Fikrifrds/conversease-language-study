# Dialogue Script

**Dimas:** Today I'd like to share an idea to improve our onboarding.
**Faris:** Great. What's the context?
**Dimas:** The problem is new hires often feel lost in the first week.
**Faris:** Okay. What's your proposal?
**Dimas:** My proposal is a simple onboarding checklist and a buddy for the first two weeks.
**Faris:** Sounds practical. What are the next steps?
**Dimas:** Next steps are: draft the checklist today, then pilot it with the next hire.
**Faris:** Nice. Let's do it.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, confident, clear
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
