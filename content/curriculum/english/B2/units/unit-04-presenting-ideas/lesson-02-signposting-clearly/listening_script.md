# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Let me quickly walk you through the plan.
**Ilham:** Sure.
**Faris:** First, I'll explain the problem. Next, I'll share the proposal. Finally, I'll outline next steps.
**Ilham:** Great. Go ahead.
**Faris:** First, the problem is that onboarding is inconsistent across teams.
**Ilham:** Okay.
**Faris:** Next, the proposal is a shared checklist and a short orientation call.
**Ilham:** And finally?
**Faris:** Finally, we can pilot it next week and collect feedback.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, confident, clear
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
