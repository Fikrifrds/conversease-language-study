# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** What are the benefits of your idea?
**Dimas:** The main benefit is faster onboarding for new hires.
**Faris:** Anything else?
**Dimas:** Another benefit is fewer repeated questions for the team.
**Faris:** Okay. Any risks?
**Dimas:** A key risk is that the checklist becomes outdated.
**Faris:** How can we reduce that risk?
**Dimas:** To reduce the risk, we can review it monthly and assign an owner.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, confident, clear
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
