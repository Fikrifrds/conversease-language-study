# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Why do you think billing complaints are up?
**Dimas:** One possible cause is the new checkout design.
**Faris:** What makes you think that?
**Dimas:** Based on the data, drop-offs increased right after the redesign.
**Faris:** Could it be something else?
**Dimas:** It might be due to slower load times as well.
**Faris:** How can we confirm?
**Dimas:** Let's compare load times and run a small A/B test.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, analytical, calm
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
