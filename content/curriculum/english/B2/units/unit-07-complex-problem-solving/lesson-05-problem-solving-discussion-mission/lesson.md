# Problem Solving Discussion Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can lead a problem-solving discussion: frame the problem, explain causes, discuss trade-offs, and recommend next steps.

## Situation

Misi: kamu pimpin diskusi problem solving dari framing sampai rekomendasi, lalu set next steps yang jelas.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
