# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Our customer complaints have increased this month.  
**Mina:** Okay. Let's define the problem statement first.  
**Faris:** Sure. Where should we start?  
**Mina:** Can we agree on the scope? Which product area is affected?  
**Faris:** Mostly the billing flow.  
**Mina:** Great. What does success look like for the next four weeks?  
**Faris:** Fewer support tickets and faster payment completion.  
**Mina:** Got it. Any constraints we should keep in mind?  
**Faris:** We can't change pricing this quarter.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, analytical, calm
- Voices: Mina and Faris
