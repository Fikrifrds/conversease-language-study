# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Our customer complaints have increased this month.
**Dimas:** Okay. Let's define the problem statement first.
**Faris:** Sure. Where should we start?
**Dimas:** Can we agree on the scope? Which product area is affected?
**Faris:** Mostly the billing flow.
**Dimas:** Great. What does success look like for the next four weeks?
**Faris:** Fewer support tickets and faster payment completion.
**Dimas:** Got it. Any constraints we should keep in mind?
**Faris:** We can't change pricing this quarter.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, analytical, calm
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
