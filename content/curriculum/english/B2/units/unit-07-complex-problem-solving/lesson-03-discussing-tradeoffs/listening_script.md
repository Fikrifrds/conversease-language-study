# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** We have two solutions. Which one should we pick?
**Dimas:** The trade-off is speed versus long-term reliability.
**Faris:** Can you explain that?
**Dimas:** If we optimize for speed, we might introduce more bugs.
**Faris:** And the slower option?
**Dimas:** On the other hand, the slower option reduces risk but takes more effort.
**Faris:** What do you suggest?
**Dimas:** Let's choose based on our biggest constraint: time or risk.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B2
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: professional, analytical, calm
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
