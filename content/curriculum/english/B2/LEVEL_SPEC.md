# English B2 - Professional Discussions

## Level Outcome

Learners can participate in structured professional and social discussions: state a clear position, support it with reasons and examples, respond to counterpoints, contribute to meetings, negotiate compromise, present ideas, evaluate information, communicate with clients, and recommend practical next steps.

## Scope

- Conversation-first English for workplace, service, presentation, negotiation, information, and problem-solving contexts.
- Indonesian explanations for adult learners.
- Lessons move from controlled response practice to guided roleplay and then more open conversation checks.
- Learner output should include position, reason, example, counterpoint response, and recommended next step.
- Feedback should reward clarity, relevance, professional tone, and repair strategies, not only grammatical correctness.

## B2 Upgrade From B1

- Dialogue length and turn density increase from connected everyday stories to structured professional discussion.
- Learners must handle disagreement, objections, client concerns, and meeting decisions.
- Grammar is taught as a communication tool: contrast linkers, conditionals, reported speech, and concise summaries.
- Reading and writing tasks support discussion skills through short professional messages, sources, and follow-up replies.

## Passing Threshold

- Overall score: 78
- Speaking / Conversation: 70
- Listening: 70
- Pronunciation: 65
- Vocabulary / Useful Phrases: 70
- Grammar: 70
- Reading: 65
- Writing: 65
- Lesson completion: 80%

Speaking and listening are critical skills. The learner cannot pass B2 if either is below its minimum score.
