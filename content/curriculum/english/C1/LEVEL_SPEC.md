# English C1 - Advanced Fluency

## Level Outcome

Learners can lead complex professional and social conversations with nuance, precision, strategic framing, persuasive structure, cross-cultural tact, and accurate response to indirect or dense speech.

## Scope

- Conversation-first English for nuanced opinions, strategic workplace communication, advanced presentations, debate, cross-cultural professionalism, leadership, coaching, and advanced listening response.
- Indonesian explanations for adult learners.
- Lessons emphasize controlled precision before open fluency: clarify the focus, model the move, practice the phrase, then use it in roleplay.
- Learner output should include qualification, uncertainty, evidence, counterpoint handling, tactful repair, and a clear final formulation.
- Feedback should evaluate nuance, register, structure, listening response, and strategic clarity alongside grammar and pronunciation.

## C1 Upgrade From B2

- Dialogue length remains practical but becomes denser, with more implied meaning and higher stakes.
- Learners must manage ambiguity, sensitive disagreement, complex presentation flow, leadership decisions, and cross-cultural repair.
- Grammar and phrase work focus on control: hedging, emphatic structures, modality, signposting, and tactful reformulation.
- Reading and writing tasks support conversation by training inference, tone, concise recommendations, and argument structure.

## Passing Threshold

- Overall score: 82
- Speaking / Conversation: 75
- Listening: 75
- Pronunciation: 70
- Vocabulary / Useful Phrases: 75
- Grammar: 75
- Reading: 70
- Writing: 70
- Lesson completion: 80%

Speaking and listening are critical skills. The learner cannot pass C1 if either is below its minimum score.
