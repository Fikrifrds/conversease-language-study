# C1 Final Test Practice

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can answer C1-style prompts with nuance, structure, and precise language under time pressure.

## Situation

Latihan final: kamu jawab prompt C1 dengan cepat tapi tetap bernuansa (frame, asumsi, trade-off, keputusan, next steps).

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
