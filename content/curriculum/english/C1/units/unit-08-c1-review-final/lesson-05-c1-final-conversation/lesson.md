# C1 Final Conversation

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can lead a complex conversation from framing to decision: nuance, debate, leadership listening, and clear next steps.

## Situation

Final: kamu memimpin percakapan kompleks yang realistis. Kamu harus frame, jawab tantangan, nangkep maksud, dan tutup dengan keputusan + next steps.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
