# Review Leadership and Listening

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can coach someone with questions, catch implied meaning, and align on a decision with clear next steps.

## Situation

Review: kamu memimpin percakapan coaching. Kamu nangkep maksud tersirat, tanya pertanyaan bagus, dan tutup dengan keputusan + next steps.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
