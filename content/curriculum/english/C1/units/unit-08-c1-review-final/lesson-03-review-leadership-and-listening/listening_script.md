# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** I feel like the team is hesitant, but they won't say it directly.
**Faris:** What I'm hearing is there's some concern under the surface.
**Ilham:** Yeah, maybe they're worried about blame.
**Faris:** When you say 'worried about blame', do you mean fear of mistakes or fear of visibility?
**Ilham:** Mostly fear of visibility.
**Faris:** What would success look like in the next two weeks?
**Ilham:** A small pilot with stable metrics.
**Faris:** Great. Next steps are: you'll propose the pilot plan, and I'll help you align stakeholders tomorrow.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: natural, confident, slightly fast
- Tone: professional, thoughtful, collaborative
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
