# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** So do you support the plan or not?
**Faris:** On balance, I support it, but I'd adjust the scope to reduce risk.
**Ilham:** What exactly would you change?
**Faris:** To be precise, I'd keep the core workflow and postpone optional add-ons.
**Ilham:** Can you commit to next week?
**Faris:** What I can commit to is a pilot next week, and a full rollout after we validate the metrics.
**Ilham:** How do we align everyone?
**Faris:** Next steps are: I'll share a one-page summary today, and we'll align in a 30-minute call tomorrow.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: natural, confident, slightly fast
- Tone: professional, thoughtful, collaborative
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
