# Review Presenting and Debate

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can present a structured argument, challenge assumptions respectfully, and respond under pressure with clarity.

## Situation

Review: kamu present argumen singkat, lalu kamu ditantang. Kamu harus jawab dengan struktur dan tetap sopan.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
