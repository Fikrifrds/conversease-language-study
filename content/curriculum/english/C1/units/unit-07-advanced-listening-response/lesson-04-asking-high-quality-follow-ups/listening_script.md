# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** We should be more cautious with the rollout.
**Faris:** When you say 'more cautious', do you mean smaller scope or slower timing?
**Ilham:** Mostly smaller scope.
**Faris:** What's your assumption about the main risk?
**Ilham:** That incidents will spike.
**Faris:** What would change your mind about the timeline?
**Ilham:** If we see stable metrics in a pilot.
**Faris:** Got it. What's the next step we should take today?
**Ilham:** Let's draft a smaller-scope plan and share it today.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: natural, slightly fast
- Tone: conversational, precise, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
