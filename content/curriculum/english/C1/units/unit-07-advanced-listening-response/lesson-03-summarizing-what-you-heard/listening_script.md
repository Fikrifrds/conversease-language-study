# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Can you summarize the meeting?
**Faris:** Sure. So, to summarize: the timeline is tight and clients are sensitive to change.
**Ilham:** Any decisions?
**Faris:** The decision is to propose a phased rollout with clear metrics.
**Ilham:** What concerns remain?
**Faris:** The open questions are around resourcing and internal alignment.
**Ilham:** Can you confirm with the team?
**Faris:** Just to confirm, I'll send the summary and ask if anything is missing.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: natural, slightly fast
- Tone: conversational, precise, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
