# Advanced Listening Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can handle advanced listening: implied meaning, long turns, accurate summary, and high-quality follow-ups.

## Situation

Misi: kamu harus menangkap maksud tersirat, merespons penjelasan panjang, merangkum akurat, dan tanya follow-up berkualitas.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
