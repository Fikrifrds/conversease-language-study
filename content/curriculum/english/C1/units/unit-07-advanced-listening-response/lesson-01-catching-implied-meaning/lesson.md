# Catching Implied Meaning

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can catch implied meaning by listening for hints, hedges, and what is not said explicitly.

## Situation

Kamu ngobrol dengan stakeholder yang ngomongnya halus/indirect. Kamu perlu nangkep maksud tersirat dan cek pemahaman.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
