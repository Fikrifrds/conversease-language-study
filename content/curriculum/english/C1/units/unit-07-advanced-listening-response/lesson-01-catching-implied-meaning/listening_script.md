**Ilham:** Well, there are a few moving parts.

**Faris:** Got it. I'll draft a phased option and share it today.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: natural, slightly fast
- Tone: conversational, precise, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
