# Responding to Long Turns

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can respond to long turns by extracting key points, acknowledging emotions, and replying in a structured way.

## Situation

Kamu dengar penjelasan panjang. Kamu harus merespons dengan rapi: rangkum poin, konfirmasi, lalu jawab.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
