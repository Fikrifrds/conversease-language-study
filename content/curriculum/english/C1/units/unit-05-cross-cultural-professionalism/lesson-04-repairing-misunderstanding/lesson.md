# Repairing Misunderstanding

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can repair a misunderstanding by acknowledging it, clarifying intent, and proposing a constructive next step.

## Situation

Terjadi miskomunikasi lintas budaya. Kamu perlu memperbaiki situasi dengan cepat, sopan, dan jelas.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
