# Reading Context

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can read context carefully by noticing tone, hierarchy, and implied expectations before responding.

## Situation

Kamu dapat pesan yang agak indirect dari stakeholder lintas budaya. Kamu perlu baca konteksnya dulu sebelum jawab.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
