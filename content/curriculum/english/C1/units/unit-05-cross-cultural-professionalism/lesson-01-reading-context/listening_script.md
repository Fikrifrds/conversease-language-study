# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** The client said the timeline is 'ambitious'. What do you think that means?
**Faris:** My sense is that they're signaling concern without saying no directly.
**Ilham:** So how should we respond?
**Faris:** Before we decide, can we clarify what their priority is—speed or risk reduction?
**Ilham:** Good point.
**Faris:** It may be worth considering a phased plan to show flexibility.
**Ilham:** And tone-wise?
**Faris:** We should stay respectful and avoid sounding defensive.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: respectful, warm, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
