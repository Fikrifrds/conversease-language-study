# Cross-cultural Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can handle a cross-cultural situation: read context, ask tactful questions, explain norms, and repair misunderstandings.

## Situation

Misi: kamu handle situasi lintas budaya dari interpretasi pesan sampai repair miskomunikasi, dengan tone yang profesional.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
