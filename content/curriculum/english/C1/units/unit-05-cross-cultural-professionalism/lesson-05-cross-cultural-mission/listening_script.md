# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** The client says the timeline is 'ambitious'.
**Faris:** My sense is that they're signaling concern indirectly. Before we decide, can we clarify whether they prioritize speed or risk reduction?
**Ilham:** They also dislike direct criticism.
**Faris:** Would you mind if I ask how they prefer to receive feedback—writing or a call?
**Ilham:** In our culture, they prefer private feedback first.
**Faris:** In our context, it's generally expected that we raise concerns one-on-one first. It might help to start with appreciation.
**Ilham:** They seemed upset after the last message.
**Faris:** I may have misunderstood their tone. Just to clarify, my intent was to confirm constraints, not reject the request. How about we send a short note and offer a quick call?
**Ilham:** Yes, let's do that. I'll set up a call.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: respectful, warm, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
