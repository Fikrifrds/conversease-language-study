# Asking Tactful Questions

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can ask tactful questions to clarify expectations, boundaries, and sensitive topics without causing discomfort.

## Situation

Kamu perlu tanya hal sensitif ke klien/stakeholder. Kamu harus menjaga sopan santun dan tetap jelas.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
