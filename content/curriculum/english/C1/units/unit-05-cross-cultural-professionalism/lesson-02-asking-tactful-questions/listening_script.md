# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** The client seems uncomfortable with direct feedback.
**Faris:** Would you mind if I ask how they prefer to receive feedback?
**Ilham:** Of course. Go ahead.
**Faris:** If it's okay, could you clarify whether they want feedback in writing or in a call?
**Ilham:** Probably in a call.
**Faris:** Just to make sure I understand, should we share concerns privately first?
**Ilham:** Yes, that would be better.
**Faris:** Great. We'll adjust our approach accordingly.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: respectful, warm, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
