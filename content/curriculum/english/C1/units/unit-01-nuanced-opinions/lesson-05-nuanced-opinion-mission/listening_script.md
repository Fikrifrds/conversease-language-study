# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Should we adopt the new policy right away?
**Faris:** To some extent, yes—especially for new projects. That said, we need exceptions for legacy systems.
**Ilham:** Are you confident it will help?
**Faris:** I'm fairly confident it will, but I'm not entirely convinced we won't see edge cases.
**Ilham:** So do we prioritize speed or stability?
**Faris:** On the one hand, speed matters. On the other hand, stability reduces risk. On balance, I'd prioritize stability with a limited rollout.
**Ilham:** I still think we should launch next week no matter what.
**Faris:** I see your point, but I'm not sure I'd go that far. I might frame it differently: limited launch next week, full rollout after we review the data.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: thoughtful, confident, precise
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
