# Nuanced Opinion Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can hold a nuanced discussion: qualify your stance, calibrate certainty, balance viewpoints, and disagree tactfully.

## Situation

Misi: kamu ikut diskusi yang menuntut opini yang nuanced. Kamu harus presisi, sopan, dan tetap decisive.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
