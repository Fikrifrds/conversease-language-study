# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** I think we should launch next week, no matter what.
**Faris:** I see your point, but I'm not sure I'd go that far.
**Ilham:** Why not?
**Faris:** With respect, the current error rate suggests we're not ready.
**Ilham:** So you want to delay?
**Faris:** I might frame it differently: we launch a limited version next week and keep the rest behind a feature flag.
**Ilham:** That could work.
**Faris:** Great—this way we protect reliability without losing momentum.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: thoughtful, confident, precise
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
