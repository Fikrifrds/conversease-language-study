# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Do you think we should adopt the new policy immediately?
**Faris:** To some extent, yes—especially for new projects.
**Ilham:** So you're fully in favor?
**Faris:** Broadly speaking, I support it. That said, we need a clear exception for legacy systems.
**Ilham:** What's the main concern?
**Faris:** In principle it's a good move, but the rollout timeline is too aggressive.
**Ilham:** So what would you suggest?
**Faris:** I'd phase it in over two quarters and review adoption data along the way.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: thoughtful, confident, precise
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
