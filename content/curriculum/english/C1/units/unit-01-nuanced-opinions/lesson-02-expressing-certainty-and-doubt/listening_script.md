# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Will this change reduce complaints?
**Faris:** I'm fairly confident it will, given the support trends.
**Ilham:** So we're safe to roll it out broadly?
**Faris:** I'm not entirely convinced. There's a strong chance we'll see edge cases.
**Ilham:** Is it risky, then?
**Faris:** It's unlikely that we'll break core flows, but we should monitor closely.
**Ilham:** What do you propose?
**Faris:** Let's start with a limited rollout and define clear success metrics.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: thoughtful, confident, precise
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
