# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** This is just speculation. Do you have anything solid?
**Dimas:** Let me be clear: we have indicators, not proof yet.
**Faris:** So why should we act now?
**Dimas:** I understand the concern. However, the key point is that waiting increases risk.
**Faris:** Show me the numbers.
**Dimas:** If you look at the last four weeks, incident volume has doubled.
**Faris:** Fine. What's your proposal?
**Dimas:** We run a pilot this week and review results before scaling.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: analytical, calm, assertive
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
