# Identifying Assumptions

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can identify assumptions behind an argument and ask clarifying questions about the premise.

## Situation

Kamu lagi debat ringan di kerja. Kamu ingin menguji argumen dengan cara mengangkat asumsi yang tersembunyi, tanpa menyerang orangnya.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
