# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** We should cut onboarding steps. Fewer steps always increase conversion.
**Dimas:** It seems you're assuming fewer steps automatically mean better conversion. What's the evidence for that claim?
**Faris:** It's common sense.
**Dimas:** I'm not sure that follows. Could there be another explanation, like unclear copy or missing reassurance?
**Faris:** Do we have data?
**Dimas:** According to the support dashboard, drop-offs increased after the redesign. To be precise, the data indicates correlation, not necessarily causation.
**Faris:** This is speculation. Why act now?
**Dimas:** Let me be clear: we have indicators, not proof yet. However, the key point is waiting increases risk. We run a pilot this week and review results before scaling.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: analytical, calm, assertive
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
