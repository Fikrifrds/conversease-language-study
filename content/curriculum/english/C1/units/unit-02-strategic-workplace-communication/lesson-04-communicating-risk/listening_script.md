# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Leadership wants the briefing tomorrow, and they expect a candid picture. What risks should we highlight to leadership?  
**Mina:** The main risk is a spike in incidents during rollout. Everything else is secondary to that.  
**Ilham:** How likely is that, realistically? They'll push back if we sound alarmist.  
**Mina:** There's a reasonable chance, given recent instability. Two of the last three deploys needed hotfixes.  
**Ilham:** And if it does happen, what's the impact in business terms?  
**Mina:** The impact would be delayed revenue and higher support load. In the worst case, roughly a two-week slip on the revenue plan.  
**Ilham:** That's significant. How do we reduce it without delaying the rollout entirely?  
**Mina:** We can mitigate it by time-boxing the rollout, adding monitoring, and having a rollback plan. I'd present it as a managed risk, not an open question.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: strategic, calm, professional
- Voices: Mina and Ilham
