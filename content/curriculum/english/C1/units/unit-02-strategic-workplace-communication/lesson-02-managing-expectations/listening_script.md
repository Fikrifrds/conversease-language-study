# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Can we deliver the full scope by next week?
**Faris:** What we can commit to is a smaller release by next week.
**Ilham:** Why not the full scope?
**Faris:** To avoid surprises, I'd rather under-promise and deliver reliably.
**Ilham:** So when can we deliver everything?
**Faris:** The earliest we can deliver the full scope is two weeks later, assuming no new blockers.
**Ilham:** Okay. What does success look like?
**Faris:** Success means fewer incidents and a stable rollout with clear monitoring.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: strategic, calm, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
