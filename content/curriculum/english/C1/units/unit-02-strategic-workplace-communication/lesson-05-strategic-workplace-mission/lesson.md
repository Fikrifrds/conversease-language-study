# Strategic Workplace Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can lead a strategic workplace conversation: align stakeholders, manage expectations, give sensitive feedback, and communicate risk.

## Situation

Misi: kamu memimpin diskusi lintas tim. Kamu selaraskan stakeholder, set ekspektasi, kasih feedback sensitif, dan jelasin risk + mitigasi.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
