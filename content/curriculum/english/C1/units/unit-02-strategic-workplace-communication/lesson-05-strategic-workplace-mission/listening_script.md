# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** We need to align on priorities and deliver next week.
**Faris:** To make sure we're aligned, what's your top priority and biggest constraint?
**Ilham:** Speed, and we can't add headcount.
**Faris:** Understood. What we can commit to is a smaller release by next week, assuming no new blockers.
**Ilham:** Some teams think it's a hard deadline.
**Faris:** I wanted to flag the impact: it was interpreted as a hard deadline. Would you be open to adding an explicit 'earliest possible' line?
**Ilham:** Yes. What risks should we mention?
**Faris:** The main risk is incidents during rollout. We can mitigate it by time-boxing, adding monitoring, and having a rollback plan.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: strategic, calm, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
