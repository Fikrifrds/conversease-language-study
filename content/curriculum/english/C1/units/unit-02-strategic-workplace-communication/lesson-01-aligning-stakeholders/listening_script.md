# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** We have conflicting priorities across teams.
**Faris:** To make sure we're aligned, can you share your top priority?
**Ilham:** Speed. We need impact this quarter.
**Faris:** Got it. From your perspective, what's the biggest constraint?
**Ilham:** We can't add headcount.
**Faris:** Understood. The key constraint is capacity. Can we agree that we ship a smaller scope first?
**Ilham:** That sounds reasonable.
**Faris:** Great. I'll capture the decision and circulate it today.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: strategic, calm, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
