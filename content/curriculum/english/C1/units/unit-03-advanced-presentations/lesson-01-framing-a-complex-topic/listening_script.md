# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Can you present the new architecture proposal?
**Dimas:** Sure. Today I'd like to walk you through the proposal and why it matters.
**Faris:** What's the key concept?
**Dimas:** By 'modular architecture', I mean we separate features into independent components.
**Faris:** And the purpose?
**Dimas:** The purpose of this is to reduce coupling and speed up delivery.
**Faris:** What should we focus on first?
**Dimas:** First, I'll outline the problem we're solving, then the proposed approach.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: confident, precise, professional
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
