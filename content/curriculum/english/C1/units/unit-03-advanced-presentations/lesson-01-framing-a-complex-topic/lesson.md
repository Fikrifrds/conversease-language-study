# Framing a Complex Topic

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can frame a complex topic by setting context, defining terms, and stating the purpose clearly.

## Situation

Kamu presentasi topik kompleks. Kamu mulai dengan konteks, definisi istilah, dan tujuan supaya audiens nggak kehilangan arah.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
