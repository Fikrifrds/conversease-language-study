# Handling Challenging Questions

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can handle challenging questions by acknowledging concerns, answering precisely, and reframing when needed.

## Situation

Saat Q&A, kamu dapat pertanyaan menantang. Kamu harus tetap tenang, jawab presisi, dan bisa reframe kalau perlu.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
