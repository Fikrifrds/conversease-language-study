# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Isn't this approach too risky? You're proposing to change the platform while three teams are in the middle of delivery.
**Dimas:** That's a fair question. Let me clarify what risk we're accepting.
**Faris:** Go ahead. I'd like to hear how you're framing it before we debate the details.
**Dimas:** The short answer is: it's manageable if we pilot first and monitor closely. The scenario that worries people is a silent failure, and the monitoring is designed to catch exactly that.
**Faris:** What if teams ignore the standards once the pilot pressure is off? That's what happened with the last initiative.
**Dimas:** What I'd emphasize is that standards need ownership and enforcement. Every guideline will have a named owner and a review cadence, not just a document.
**Faris:** Understood. And if it still goes wrong, what's the fallback?
**Dimas:** If adoption stalls, we pause expansion and revisit the design. The pilot is deliberately cheap to stop, so there's no sunk-cost pressure.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: confident, precise, professional
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
