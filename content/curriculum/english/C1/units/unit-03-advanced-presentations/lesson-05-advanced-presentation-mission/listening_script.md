# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Mission: present the proposal and handle questions.
**Dimas:** Today I'd like to walk you through the proposal and why it matters. By 'modular architecture', I mean independent components.
**Faris:** What's your main claim?
**Dimas:** The core claim is that this will improve delivery speed. The evidence suggests teams move faster with clear ownership.
**Faris:** Isn't it too risky?
**Dimas:** That's a fair question. The short answer is: it's manageable if we pilot first and monitor closely.
**Faris:** So what's crucial?
**Dimas:** What's crucial here is shared standards. With that in mind, I'd propose a time-boxed pilot with guardrails.
**Faris:** Sounds good. Any final summary?
**Dimas:** In short, the benefits outweigh the risks if we set guardrails early. Next steps are: I'll share the plan today, and you'll review it by Friday.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: confident, precise, professional
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
