# Advanced Presentation Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can deliver an advanced presentation and handle challenging questions with clear signposting and strategic answers.

## Situation

Misi: kamu presentasi topik kompleks dengan alur yang meyakinkan, transisi rapi, dan Q&A yang tenang.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
