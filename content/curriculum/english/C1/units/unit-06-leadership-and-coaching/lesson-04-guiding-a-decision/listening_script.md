# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** We can't decide which approach to take.
**Faris:** We have three options: pilot, phased rollout, or full rollout.
**Ilham:** What's the trade-off?
**Faris:** The trade-off is speed versus risk and operational load.
**Ilham:** Given our constraints, what do you recommend?
**Faris:** Given our constraints, I'd recommend a phased rollout with clear monitoring.
**Ilham:** Okay. Can we align on next steps?
**Faris:** Yes—can we agree on the scope today and review metrics weekly?
**Ilham:** Yes, let's do that. We'll align on scope today and review metrics weekly.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: calm, encouraging, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
