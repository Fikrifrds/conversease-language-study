# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** How was my update message?
**Faris:** Overall it's solid. One thing I'd suggest is leading with the decision.
**Ilham:** Why?
**Faris:** The impact is that stakeholders scan quickly and might miss the point.
**Ilham:** So what should I change?
**Faris:** A concrete improvement would be a one-line summary at the top.
**Ilham:** Okay.
**Faris:** Would you be open to revising it and sending a second draft?
**Ilham:** Yes, I'll revise it and send it today.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: calm, encouraging, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
