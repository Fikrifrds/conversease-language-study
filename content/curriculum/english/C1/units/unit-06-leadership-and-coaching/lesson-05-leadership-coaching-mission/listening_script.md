# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** I'm stuck and we need to decide fast.
**Faris:** The direction I'd like to set is stabilizing billing first. What options do you see?
**Ilham:** Pilot or phased rollout.
**Faris:** Great. What would success look like? And what's the smallest next step today?
**Ilham:** Draft the checklist and metrics.
**Faris:** Overall your update is solid. One thing I'd suggest is leading with the decision—the impact is people might miss it.
**Ilham:** Got it. What do you recommend?
**Faris:** Given our constraints, I'd recommend a phased rollout. Can we agree on scope today and review metrics weekly?
**Ilham:** Yes, let's align on scope today and review metrics weekly.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: calm, encouraging, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
