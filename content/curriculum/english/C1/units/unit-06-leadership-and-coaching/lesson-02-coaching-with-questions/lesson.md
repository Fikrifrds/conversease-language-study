# Coaching with Questions

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can coach others using questions that build ownership, clarify thinking, and unblock decisions.

## Situation

Kamu coaching teammate. Alih-alih kasih jawaban, kamu pakai pertanyaan yang membantu mereka berpikir dan ambil ownership.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
