# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** I'm stuck on how to handle the rollout.
**Faris:** What options do you see right now?
**Ilham:** We can do a pilot or a full rollout.
**Faris:** What would success look like for the pilot?
**Ilham:** Low incidents and clear data.
**Faris:** What's the smallest next step you can take today?
**Ilham:** Draft the rollout checklist.
**Faris:** Great. What support do you need from me?
**Ilham:** Can you review it and help me align with stakeholders?
**Faris:** Yes. Send it to me today, and I'll help you align tomorrow.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: calm, encouraging, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
