# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** What should the team focus on this sprint?

**Ilham:** How do we define success?
**Faris:** Success looks like fewer incidents and faster completion rates.
**Ilham:** Who owns what?

**Ilham:** Any constraints?
**Faris:** Yes—let's align on scope and keep it time-boxed to two weeks.

## Audio Direction

- Level: C1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: calm, encouraging, professional
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
