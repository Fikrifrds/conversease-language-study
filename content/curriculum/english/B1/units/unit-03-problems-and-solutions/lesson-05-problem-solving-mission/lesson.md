# Problem Solving Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can complete a mini problem-solving conversation: describe a problem, suggest a solution, respond to a concern, and decide next steps.

## Situation

Misi: kamu jelaskan problem, kasih solusi, tanggapi concern, lalu putuskan next step.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
