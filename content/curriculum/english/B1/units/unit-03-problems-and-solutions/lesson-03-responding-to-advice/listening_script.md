# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** I'm stressed because I keep missing my speaking practice.
**Hilmi:** Maybe you should set a small daily goal.
**Faris:** That sounds good. What do you suggest?
**Hilmi:** Try five minutes every morning.
**Faris:** You're right. I'll try that starting tomorrow.
**Hilmi:** And maybe track it in a checklist.
**Faris:** Good idea. Thanks.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Hilmi and Faris
