# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** We have two options: fix it now or wait until tomorrow.
**Faris:** I'd rather fix it now because users are blocked.
**Hilmi:** True. But it's late.
**Faris:** We could do a quick rollback and then sleep.
**Hilmi:** That sounds reasonable.
**Faris:** Okay, let's roll back now and do a full review tomorrow.
**Hilmi:** Deal.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
