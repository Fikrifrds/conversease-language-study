# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hey Dimas. You look concerned. What's going on?
**Dimas:** There's a problem with the login page.
**Faris:** What happened?
**Dimas:** It started this morning when we deployed the update.
**Faris:** How bad is it?
**Dimas:** Users can't sign in, so they can't access their lessons.
**Faris:** Okay. Let's fix it quickly.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
