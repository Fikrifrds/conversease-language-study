# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hi Dimas. What are you working on today?
**Dimas:** I'm working on the onboarding email flow.
**Faris:** Great. What's the next step?
**Dimas:** Next, I'll review the copy and update the templates.
**Faris:** Sounds good. Any blockers?
**Dimas:** Not right now, but I may need feedback later.
**Faris:** Okay. Keep me posted.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
