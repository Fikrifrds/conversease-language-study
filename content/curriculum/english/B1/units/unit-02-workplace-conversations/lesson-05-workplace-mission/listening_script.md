# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hi Dimas. Quick update on the report?
**Dimas:** I'm making good progress. I'm almost done with the summary.
**Faris:** Great. Please update the risk section too.
**Dimas:** Sure. Could you clarify which risks you want me to focus on?
**Faris:** Focus on timeline and budget risks.
**Dimas:** Got it. Just to confirm, you need it by Friday morning, right?
**Faris:** Yes. Please send it by Thursday afternoon if possible.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Dimas
