# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Can you update the report for Friday?  
**Mina:** Sure. Could you clarify which sections you need?  
**Faris:** Focus on the summary and the risks.  
**Mina:** Got it. Just to confirm, you want the latest numbers too, right?  
**Faris:** Yes, please include the latest numbers.  
**Mina:** Okay. I'll send it by Thursday afternoon.  
**Faris:** Perfect. Thanks.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Mina
