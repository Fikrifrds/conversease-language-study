# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** So, what's your neighborhood like?
**Faris:** I live in a quiet neighborhood. It's known for food stalls.
**Ilham:** Nice. Any local habits?
**Faris:** People usually eat outside in the evening, especially on weekends.
**Ilham:** Interesting. What is it like in your country during big holidays?
**Faris:** It's busy, but it's also meaningful. Mostly we spend time with family.
**Ilham:** In my country, people eat dinner early, around 6 p.m.
**Faris:** Oh, that's interesting. I'm not used to that, but it sounds nice.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, respectful
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
