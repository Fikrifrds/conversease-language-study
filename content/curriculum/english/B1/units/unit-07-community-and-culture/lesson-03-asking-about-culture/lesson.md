# Asking About Culture

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can ask about cultural traditions politely and respond with a short explanation.

## Situation

Kamu ngobrol sama orang baru. Kamu tanya tradisi atau kebiasaan di negaranya dengan sopan, lalu jawab singkat tentang negaramu juga.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
