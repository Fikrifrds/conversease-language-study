# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Do you have any big holidays in your country?
**Faris:** Yes. We celebrate Eid, and many people travel to visit family.
**Ilham:** What is it like during that time?
**Faris:** It's busy, but it's also warm and meaningful.
**Ilham:** Do people usually give gifts?
**Faris:** Sometimes. Mostly we share food and spend time together.
**Ilham:** That sounds lovely.
**Faris:** How about in your country?
**Ilham:** We have a big holiday too. People visit family and share food.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, respectful
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
