# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** Are there any local habits in your area?
**Faris:** Yeah. People usually eat outside in the evening.
**Hilmi:** Interesting. Like street food?
**Faris:** Exactly. In my area, people often grab noodles or satay after work.
**Hilmi:** Do families do that too?
**Faris:** Yes, especially on weekends.
**Hilmi:** That sounds fun.
**Faris:** It is. It's a social thing.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, respectful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
