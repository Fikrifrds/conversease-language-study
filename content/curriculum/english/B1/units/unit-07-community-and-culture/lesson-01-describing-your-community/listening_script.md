# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** So, where do you live?
**Faris:** I live in a quiet neighborhood near the city center.
**Hilmi:** Nice. What's it like?
**Faris:** It's known for its food stalls and small parks.
**Hilmi:** Sounds great. What do you like about it?
**Faris:** I like it because it's convenient but still peaceful.
**Hilmi:** Do you know your neighbors?
**Faris:** Yeah, we say hi and help each other sometimes.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, respectful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
