# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** In my country, people usually eat dinner early, around 6 p.m.
**Faris:** Oh, that's interesting. In my country, we often eat later.
**Ilham:** Really? Around what time?
**Faris:** Around 8 or 9 p.m. I'm not used to eating that early.
**Ilham:** That makes sense.
**Faris:** But eating early sounds nice, especially if you sleep early.
**Ilham:** Yeah, it works well for families.
**Faris:** Cool. Thanks for sharing.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, respectful
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
