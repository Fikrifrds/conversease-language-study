# Being Polite With Differences

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can talk about cultural differences politely and show respect even if you do things differently.

## Situation

Kamu diskusi perbedaan kebiasaan. Kamu jelasin kebiasaanmu, respon dengan sopan, dan tunjukkan respek.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
