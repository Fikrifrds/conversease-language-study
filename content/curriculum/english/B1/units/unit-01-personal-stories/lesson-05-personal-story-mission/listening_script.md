# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Tell me about something interesting that happened recently.
**Hilmi:** Last weekend, I was in Bandung with my cousin.
**Faris:** Okay. What happened?
**Hilmi:** First, we explored the city. Then we got lost for a while.
**Faris:** Oh no. How did you feel?
**Hilmi:** I felt nervous, but after we asked for directions, (sighs) I felt relieved.
**Faris:** What happened next?
**Hilmi:** Finally, we found a great place to eat and ended the day happily.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
