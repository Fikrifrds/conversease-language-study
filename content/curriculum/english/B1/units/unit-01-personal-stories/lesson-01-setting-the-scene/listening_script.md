# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** You look happy today. What happened?
**Hilmi:** Last weekend, I was in Bandung with my cousin.
**Faris:** Oh nice. What were you doing there?
**Hilmi:** We were visiting my aunt and exploring the city.
**Faris:** How was the place?
**Hilmi:** It was cooler than Jakarta, and the food was great.
**Faris:** Sounds like a good trip.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
