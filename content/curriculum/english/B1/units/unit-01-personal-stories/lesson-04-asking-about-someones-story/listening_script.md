# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** So you got lost. What happened next?  
**Hilmi:** We asked a local person for help.  
**Mina:** Why did you choose that route?  
**Hilmi:** Because my cousin thought it was faster.  
**Mina:** How did you feel at that moment?  
**Hilmi:** I felt worried, but then I felt relieved.  
**Mina:** I'm glad it worked out.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Hilmi
