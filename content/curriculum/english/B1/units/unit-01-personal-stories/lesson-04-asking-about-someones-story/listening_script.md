# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** So you got lost. What happened next?
**Hilmi:** We asked a local person for help.
**Faris:** Why did you choose that route?
**Hilmi:** Because my cousin thought it was faster.
**Faris:** How did you feel at that moment?
**Hilmi:** I felt worried, but then I felt relieved.
**Faris:** I'm glad it worked out.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
