# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** So what did you do on your trip?
**Hilmi:** First, we checked in at the hotel.
**Faris:** Okay. Then what?
**Hilmi:** Then we walked around and tried street food.
**Faris:** Nice. What happened after that?
**Hilmi:** After that, we met my aunt for dinner.
**Faris:** And finally?
**Hilmi:** (sighs) Finally, we went back early because we were tired.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
