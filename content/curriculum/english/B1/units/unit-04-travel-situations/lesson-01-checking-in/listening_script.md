# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Welcome. How can I help you?
**Faris:** Hi. I'd like to check in.
**Ilham:** Sure. Do you have a reservation?
**Faris:** Yes. I have a reservation under Faris Kim.
**Ilham:** Great. Could I see your ID, please?
**Faris:** Of course. Here you go.
**Ilham:** Thank you. Breakfast is included. Check-out is at 11 a.m.
**Faris:** Perfect. Thanks for your help.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
