# Travel Situation Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can complete a travel mini-conversation: explain a delay, check in, ask for a recommendation, and request help with a small issue.

## Situation

Misi: kamu telat sampai hotel, check-in, minta rekomendasi makan, lalu minta bantuan untuk masalah kecil di kamar.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
