# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Hi Faris. Are you on your way to the hotel?
**Faris:** Yes, but I'm running a bit late. My train is delayed.
**Ilham:** No worries. When will you arrive?
**Faris:** I'll be there in about 20 minutes.
**Ilham:** Okay. When you arrive, we'll check you in.
**Faris:** Thanks. I have a reservation under Faris Kim.
**Ilham:** Great. Also, do you need anything else?
**Faris:** Yes. Do you have any recommendations for dinner? And if there's a problem with my room, could you help me?
**Ilham:** Sure. I recommend the restaurant next to the hotel. And yes, we can help you anytime.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
