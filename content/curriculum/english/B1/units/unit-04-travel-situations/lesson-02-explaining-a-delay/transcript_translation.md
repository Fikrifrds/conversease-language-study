# Transcript Translation

- **Ilham:** Hi Faris. Are you on your way? -> Hai Faris. Kamu lagi di jalan?
- **Faris:** Yes, but I'm running a bit late. -> Iya, tapi aku agak telat.
- **Ilham:** Oh no. What happened? -> Waduh. Kenapa?
- **Faris:** My train is delayed because of a signal problem. -> Kereta aku delay karena masalah sinyal.
- **Ilham:** Okay. When will you arrive? -> Oke. Kamu sampai jam berapa?
- **Faris:** I'll be there in about 20 minutes. -> Aku sampai kira-kira 20 menit lagi.
- **Ilham:** No problem. We can start a little later. -> Nggak apa-apa. Kita bisa mulai agak telat sedikit.
- **Faris:** Thank you for waiting. -> Makasih sudah nungguin ya.
