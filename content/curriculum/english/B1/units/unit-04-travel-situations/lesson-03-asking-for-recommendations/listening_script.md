# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hi. Do you have any recommendations for dinner?
**Ilham:** Sure. Are you looking for something local?
**Faris:** Yes. Something local would be great.
**Ilham:** I recommend Sate House. It's nearby and popular.
**Faris:** Sounds good. Is it far from here?
**Ilham:** Not at all. It's a 10-minute walk.
**Faris:** Great. How do I get there?
**Ilham:** Go straight and turn left at the second corner.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
