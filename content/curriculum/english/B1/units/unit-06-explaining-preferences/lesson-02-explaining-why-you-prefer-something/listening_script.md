# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** So which one do you prefer?
**Dimas:** I prefer the earlier flight.
**Faris:** Why?
**Dimas:** Because it gives us more time in the afternoon.
**Faris:** That makes sense. Any other reasons?
**Dimas:** The main reason is I don't want to arrive too late.
**Faris:** Okay, let's book it.
**Dimas:** Great.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
