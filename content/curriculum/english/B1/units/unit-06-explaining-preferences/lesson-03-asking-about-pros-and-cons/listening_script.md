# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** What are the pros and cons of working remotely?  
**Hilmi:** The advantage is you save commuting time.  
**Mina:** True. Any downsides?  
**Hilmi:** One downside is you might feel isolated.  
**Mina:** Yeah, I get that.  
**Hilmi:** Overall, it depends on your work style.  
**Mina:** Makes sense.  
**Hilmi:** What's your preference?  
**Mina:** I prefer a mix. I work remotely most days.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Hilmi
