# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** What are the pros and cons of working remotely?
**Hilmi:** The advantage is you save commuting time.
**Faris:** True. Any downsides?
**Hilmi:** One downside is you might feel isolated.
**Faris:** Yeah, I get that.
**Hilmi:** Overall, it depends on your work style.
**Faris:** Makes sense.
**Hilmi:** What's your preference?
**Faris:** I prefer a mix. I work remotely most days.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
