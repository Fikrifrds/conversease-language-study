# Asking About Pros and Cons

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can ask about pros and cons and respond with one advantage and one downside.

## Situation

Kamu lagi diskusi pilihan (misalnya remote vs office). Kamu tanya pros and cons, lalu respon dengan satu kelebihan dan satu kekurangan.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
