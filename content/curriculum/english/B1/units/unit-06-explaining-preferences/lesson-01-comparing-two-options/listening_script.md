# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** We have two options for dinner: Sushi Place or Noodle House.
**Hilmi:** Okay. What's the difference?
**Faris:** Sushi Place is nicer, but it's more expensive.
**Hilmi:** And Noodle House?
**Faris:** It's cheaper and faster, but it's usually crowded.
**Hilmi:** Hmm. Which do you prefer?
**Faris:** I think I prefer Noodle House tonight.
**Hilmi:** Sounds good to me.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
