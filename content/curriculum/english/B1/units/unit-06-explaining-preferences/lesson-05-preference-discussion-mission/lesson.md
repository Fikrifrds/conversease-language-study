# Preference Discussion Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can complete a mini conversation: compare options, explain your preference with reasons, discuss pros and cons, and reach an agreement.

## Situation

Misi: kamu diskusi pilihan bareng teman. Kamu bandingin opsi, jelasin preferensi dan alasan, bahas pro/kontra, lalu sepakat.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
