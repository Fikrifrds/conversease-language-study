# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** We have two options: eat out or cook at home.
**Faris:** Eating out is easier, but it's more expensive.
**Hilmi:** So what do you prefer?
**Faris:** I prefer cooking at home because it's cheaper.
**Hilmi:** What are the pros and cons of cooking?
**Faris:** The advantage is it's healthier. One downside is it takes time.
**Hilmi:** Okay. How about we cook something simple?
**Faris:** That works for me. So we agree on cooking at home?
**Hilmi:** Yes, let's cook at home.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
