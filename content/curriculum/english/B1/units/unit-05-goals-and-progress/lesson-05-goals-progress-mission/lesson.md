# Goals Progress Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can complete a mini conversation: state a goal, share progress, describe a challenge, and set a next-step plan.

## Situation

Misi: kamu cerita goal kamu, update progress, sebut tantangan, lalu bikin next step plan yang realistis.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
