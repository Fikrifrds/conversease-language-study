# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** What's your goal right now?
**Faris:** My goal is to speak more confidently by the end of this month.
**Hilmi:** Nice. How's it going?
**Faris:** I'm making progress. I've been practicing every morning.
**Hilmi:** Any challenges?
**Faris:** The biggest challenge is staying consistent after work. I get distracted by my phone.
**Hilmi:** Got it. What's your next step?
**Faris:** My next step is to practice with Conversation Coach three times this week.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
