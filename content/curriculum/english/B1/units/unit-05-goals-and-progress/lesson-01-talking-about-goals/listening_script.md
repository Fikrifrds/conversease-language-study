# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** So, what's your goal this month?
**Faris:** My goal is to speak more confidently.
**Hilmi:** Nice. By when?
**Faris:** By the end of this month.
**Hilmi:** Why is that important to you?
**Faris:** Because I want to join meetings without feeling nervous.
**Hilmi:** That sounds great. How will you practice?
**Faris:** I'll practice five minutes every day.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
