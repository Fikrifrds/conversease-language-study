# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** So what's your next step?
**Faris:** My next step is to practice with Conversation Coach.
**Hilmi:** Nice. When will you do it?
**Faris:** I'll do it three times this week.
**Hilmi:** Great. Which days?
**Faris:** Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.
**Hilmi:** Sounds realistic.
**Faris:** Yeah. I'm going to keep it simple.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
