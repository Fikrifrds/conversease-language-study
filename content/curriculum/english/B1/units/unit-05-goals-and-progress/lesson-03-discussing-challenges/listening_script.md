# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** How is your practice going this week?  
**Mina:** Honestly, it's been challenging.  
**Hilmi:** What's the biggest challenge?  
**Mina:** The biggest challenge is staying consistent after work.  
**Hilmi:** Why is it hard?  
**Mina:** I get distracted by my phone, and I feel tired.  
**Hilmi:** I see. Do you want a quick tip?  
**Mina:** Yes, please. Do you have any tips?  
**Hilmi:** Sure. Try a 10-minute timer and put your phone away.  

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Mina and Hilmi
