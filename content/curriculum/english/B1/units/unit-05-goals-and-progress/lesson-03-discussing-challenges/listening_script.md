# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** How is your practice going this week?
**Faris:** Honestly, it's been challenging.
**Hilmi:** What's the biggest challenge?
**Faris:** The biggest challenge is staying consistent after work.
**Hilmi:** Why is it hard?
**Faris:** I get distracted by my phone, and I feel tired.
**Hilmi:** I see. Do you want a quick tip?
**Faris:** Yes, please. Do you have any tips?
**Hilmi:** Sure. Try a 10-minute timer and put your phone away.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
