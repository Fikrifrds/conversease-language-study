# Explaining Progress

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can explain your progress clearly, mention what improved, and say what you still need to work on.

## Situation

Kamu kasih update progress ke teman. Kamu bilang progressnya gimana, apa yang membaik, dan apa yang masih perlu dilatih.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
