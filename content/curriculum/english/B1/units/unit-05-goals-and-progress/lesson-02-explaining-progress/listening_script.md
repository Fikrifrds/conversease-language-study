# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** How's your speaking goal going?
**Faris:** I'm making good progress.
**Hilmi:** Nice. What have you been doing?
**Faris:** I've been practicing every morning for five minutes.
**Hilmi:** How far are you now?
**Faris:** I'm about halfway. I can speak for two minutes without stopping.
**Hilmi:** That's great. What still needs work?
**Faris:** I still need to improve my pronunciation.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, helpful
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
