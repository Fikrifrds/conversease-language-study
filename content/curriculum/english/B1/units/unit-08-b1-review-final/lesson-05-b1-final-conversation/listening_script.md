# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** How have you been lately?
**Faris:** Pretty good. Last week I tried a new speaking routine.
**Ilham:** Nice. What happened?
**Faris:** At first it was hard, but then it got easier because I kept sessions short.
**Ilham:** Great progress. What's your goal now?
**Faris:** My goal is to speak more confidently by next month.
**Ilham:** Any problems right now?
**Faris:** Sometimes I get distracted, so I leave my phone in another room.
**Ilham:** Good idea. Which practice method do you prefer?
**Faris:** I prefer Conversation Coach because it gives feedback. So my next step is to practice three times this week.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
