# B1 Final Conversation

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can hold a connected conversation that includes a story, a plan, a problem, a preference, and a next step.

## Situation

Final conversation: kamu ngobrol panjang (tetap sederhana) mencakup cerita, goal, problem, preferensi, dan kesepakatan next step.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to short prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
