# Dialogue Script

**Ilham:** Quick check: what's the issue?
**Faris:** There's a problem with the meeting link.
**Ilham:** What's the impact?
**Faris:** People can't join, so we need a new link.
**Ilham:** Okay. Are you on your way?
**Faris:** Yes, but I'm running a bit late. My train is delayed.
**Ilham:** When will you arrive?
**Faris:** I'll be there in about 15 minutes.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: Faris and Ilham
