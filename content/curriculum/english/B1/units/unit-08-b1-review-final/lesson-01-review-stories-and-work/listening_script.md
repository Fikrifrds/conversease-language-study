# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** How was your weekend?
**Dimas:** It was great. I visited my cousin, and we went to a small concert.
**Faris:** Nice. What happened after that?
**Dimas:** Then we grabbed street food and talked until late.
**Faris:** Sounds fun. How's work going today?
**Dimas:** I'm working on the release checklist. I'm making good progress.
**Faris:** Great. What's the next step?
**Dimas:** Next, I'll review the risks and share an update with the team.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: Dimas and Faris
