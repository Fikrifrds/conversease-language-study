# Dialogue Script

**Hilmi:** What's your goal right now?
**Faris:** My goal is to speak more confidently by the end of this month.
**Hilmi:** How's it going?
**Faris:** I'm making progress. I've been practicing every morning.
**Hilmi:** Nice. What do you prefer for practice: coach or shadowing?
**Faris:** I prefer Conversation Coach because it gives me feedback.
**Hilmi:** That makes sense.
**Faris:** Yeah, it helps me stay consistent.

## Audio Direction

- Level: B1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: Faris and Hilmi
