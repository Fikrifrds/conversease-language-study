# Making Simple Requests

## Conversation Goal

Learners can make a simple request with please and respond to yes or no.

## Situation Setup

Kamu sedang di kelas online. Kamu perlu meminta link dan meminta teman menunggu sebentar.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada cara menjelaskan masalah, meminta bantuan, dan merespons dengan sopan.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Say the problem or confusion clearly.
2. Ask for help or repetition.
3. Confirm the instruction or request.
4. Respond when the problem is fixed.
5. Close with thanks or apology.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Can you send me the link, please?"
