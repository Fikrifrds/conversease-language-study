# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Can you send me the link, please?
**Ben:** Yes, of course.
**Faris:** Thank you.
**Ben:** Can you wait a minute?
**Faris:** Sure. No problem.
**Ben:** Here is the link.
**Faris:** Great. Thanks.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: calm, helpful, supportive
- Voices: one female learner, one male learner
