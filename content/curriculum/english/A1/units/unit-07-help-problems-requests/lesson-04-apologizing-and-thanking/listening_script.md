# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Sorry I'm late.
**Sara:** That's okay.
**Ben:** My internet was slow.
**Sara:** No problem. Please join the class.
**Ben:** Thank you for waiting.
**Sara:** You're welcome.
**Ben:** I am ready now.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: calm, helpful, supportive
- Voices: one male learner, one female teacher
