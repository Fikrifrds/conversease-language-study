# Apologizing and Thanking

## Conversation Goal

Learners can apologize for a small problem and thank someone for help.

## Situation Setup

Kamu terlambat masuk kelas online karena masalah koneksi. Minta maaf dan ucapkan terima kasih ketika dibantu.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada cara menjelaskan masalah, meminta bantuan, dan merespons dengan sopan.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Say the problem or confusion clearly.
2. Ask for help or repetition.
3. Confirm the instruction or request.
4. Respond when the problem is fixed.
5. Close with thanks or apology.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Sorry I'm late."
