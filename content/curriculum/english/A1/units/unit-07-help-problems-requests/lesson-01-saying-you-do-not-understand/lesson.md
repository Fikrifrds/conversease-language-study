# Saying You Do Not Understand

## Conversation Goal

Learners can say they do not understand and ask for repetition politely.

## Situation Setup

Kamu sedang belajar online dan tidak paham satu instruksi. Katakan dengan sopan bahwa kamu belum mengerti.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada cara menjelaskan masalah, meminta bantuan, dan merespons dengan sopan.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Say the problem or confusion clearly.
2. Ask for help or repetition.
3. Confirm the instruction or request.
4. Respond when the problem is fixed.
5. Close with thanks or apology.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Sorry, I don't understand."
