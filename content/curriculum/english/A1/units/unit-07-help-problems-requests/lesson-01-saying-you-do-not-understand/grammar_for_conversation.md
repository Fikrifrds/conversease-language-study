# Grammar for Conversation: I don't understand

Grammar di lesson ini membantu learner meminta bantuan dan menjelaskan masalah secara singkat.

## Use 'I don't + verb' for a negative statement.

- Example: I don't understand.

## Use 'Can you + verb?' for a polite request.

- Example: Can you repeat that, please?

## Use a short question to confirm.

- Example: Open my book?
