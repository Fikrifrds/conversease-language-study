# Writing Support

Write short lines:

1. Write your name.
2. Spell your name.
3. Write a phone number and email address in spoken form.
4. Confirm that everything is correct.

Example:

```txt
My name is Dimas.
D-I-M-A-S.
Zero eight one two, three four five six.
Dimas at example dot com.
Everything is correct.
```
