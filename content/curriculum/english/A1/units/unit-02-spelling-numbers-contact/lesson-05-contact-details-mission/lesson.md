# Contact Details Mission

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can share your name, spelling, phone number, and email address in one simple conversation.

## Situation

Ini adalah misi akhir unit. Kamu memberikan detail kontak lengkap untuk pendaftaran kelas: nama, ejaan nama, nomor telepon, dan alamat email.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful contact-detail phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to simple prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
