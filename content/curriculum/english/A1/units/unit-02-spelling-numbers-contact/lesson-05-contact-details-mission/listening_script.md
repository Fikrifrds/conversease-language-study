# Dialogue Script

**Officer:** Hi. I need your contact details.
**Dimas:** Sure. My name is Dimas.
**Officer:** How do you spell your name?
**Dimas:** D-I-M-A-S.
**Officer:** What is your phone number?
**Dimas:** It's zero eight one two, three four five six, seven eight nine zero.
**Officer:** And your email address?
**Dimas:** It's dimas at example dot com.
**Officer:** Thank you. Is everything correct?
**Dimas:** Yes, everything is correct.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female officer, one male learner
