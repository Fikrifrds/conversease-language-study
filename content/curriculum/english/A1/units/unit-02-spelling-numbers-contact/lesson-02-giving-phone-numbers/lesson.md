# Giving Phone Numbers

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can give a phone number clearly and confirm it when someone reads it back.

## Situation

Kamu mengisi formulir kelas online. Petugas meminta nomor teleponmu. Sebutkan angka dengan pelan, lalu konfirmasi saat petugas membacanya kembali.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful contact-detail phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to simple prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
