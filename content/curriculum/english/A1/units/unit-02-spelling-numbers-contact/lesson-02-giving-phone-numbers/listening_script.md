# Dialogue Script

**Officer:** What is your phone number?
**Dimas:** It's zero eight one two, three four five six, seven eight nine zero.
**Officer:** Let me check. Zero eight one two, three four five six, seven eight nine zero?
**Dimas:** Yes, that's correct.
**Officer:** Thank you. We will send you a message.
**Dimas:** Thank you.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female officer, one male learner
