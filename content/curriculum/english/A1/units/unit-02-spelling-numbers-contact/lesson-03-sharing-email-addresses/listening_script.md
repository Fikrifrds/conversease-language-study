# Dialogue Script

**Officer:** What is your email address?
**Ben:** It's ben dot rama at example dot com.
**Officer:** Can you spell that, please?
**Ben:** B-E-N dot R-A-M-A at example dot com.
**Officer:** Thank you. Is that correct?
**Ben:** Yes, that's correct.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female officer, one male learner
