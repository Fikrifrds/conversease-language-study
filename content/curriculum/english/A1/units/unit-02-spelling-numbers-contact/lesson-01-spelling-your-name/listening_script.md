# Dialogue Script

**Officer:** Hi. What is your name?  
**Dimas:** My name is Dimas.  
**Officer:** How do you spell it?  
**Dimas:** D-I-M-A-S. Dimas.  
**Officer:** Thank you. Let me read it back. D-I-M-A-S.  
**Dimas:** That's right.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female, one male
