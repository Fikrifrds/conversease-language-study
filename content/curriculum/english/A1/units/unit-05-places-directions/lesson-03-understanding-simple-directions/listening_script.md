# Dialogue Script

**Omar:** Where is the meeting room?
**Ben:** Go straight.
**Omar:** Okay. Go straight.
**Ben:** Then turn left.
**Omar:** Turn left?
**Ben:** Yes. The room is on the right.
**Omar:** Thank you. I understand.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female learner, one male learner
