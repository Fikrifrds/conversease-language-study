# Understanding Simple Directions

## Conversation Goal

Learners can understand very simple directions with go straight, turn left, and turn right.

## Situation Setup

Kamu mencari ruang meeting di gedung. Temanmu memberi arahan sederhana.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada pertanyaan tempat, arah sederhana, dan cara memastikan informasi.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Start politely.
2. Ask where the place is or how to get there.
3. Listen for place words, floor, landmark, or direction steps.
4. Repeat or confirm the key direction.
5. Close with thanks.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Go straight."
