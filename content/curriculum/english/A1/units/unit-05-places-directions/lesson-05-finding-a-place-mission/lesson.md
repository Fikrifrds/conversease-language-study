# Finding a Place Mission

## Conversation Goal

Learners can complete a short place-finding conversation from question to confirmation.

## Situation Setup

Kamu perlu menemukan ruang kelas, lalu memastikan arah dengan orang lain secara sopan.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada pertanyaan tempat, arah sederhana, dan cara memastikan informasi.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Start politely.
2. Ask where the place is or how to get there.
3. Listen for place words, floor, landmark, or direction steps.
4. Repeat or confirm the key direction.
5. Close with thanks.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "How do I get to room A?"
