# Grammar for Conversation: combining direction steps

Grammar di lesson ini hanya dipakai untuk membantu percakapan, bukan untuk hafalan panjang.

## Use 'and' for two simple actions.

- Example: Go straight and turn left.

## Use 'Is it...?' to confirm.

- Example: Is it on the first floor?

## Use 'next to' for a nearby landmark.

- Example: Room A is next to the office.
