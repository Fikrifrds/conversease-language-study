# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Excuse me. How do I get to room A?
**Sara:** Go straight and turn left.
**Ben:** Go straight and turn left.
**Sara:** Yes. Room A is next to the office.
**Ben:** Is it on the first floor?
**Sara:** Yes, it is.
**Ben:** Great. Thank you.
**Sara:** You're welcome.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one male learner, one female learner
