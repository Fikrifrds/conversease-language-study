# Dialogue Script

**Dimas:** How do I get to the station?
**Faris:** Go straight for two minutes.
**Dimas:** Okay. Go straight for two minutes.
**Faris:** Then turn right at the bank.
**Dimas:** Turn right at the bank?
**Faris:** Yes. The station is there.
**Dimas:** Thank you for your help.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one male learner, one female learner
