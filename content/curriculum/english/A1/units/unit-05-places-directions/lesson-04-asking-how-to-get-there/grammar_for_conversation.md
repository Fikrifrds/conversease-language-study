# Grammar for Conversation: How do I get to...?

Grammar di lesson ini hanya dipakai untuk membantu percakapan, bukan untuk hafalan panjang.

## Ask 'How do I get to + place?'

- Example: How do I get to the station?

## Use 'for + time' for duration.

- Example: Go straight for two minutes.

## Use 'at + place' for a landmark.

- Example: Turn right at the bank.
