# Asking How to Get There

## Conversation Goal

Learners can ask how to get to a place and follow two simple steps.

## Situation Setup

Kamu ingin ke stasiun dari area kursus. Kamu meminta arahan yang sangat singkat.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada pertanyaan tempat, arah sederhana, dan cara memastikan informasi.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Start politely.
2. Ask where the place is or how to get there.
3. Listen for place words, floor, landmark, or direction steps.
4. Repeat or confirm the key direction.
5. Close with thanks.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "How do I get to the station?"
