# Transcript Translation

- **Dimas:** How do I get to the station? -> Bagaimana saya ke stasiun?
- **Mina:** Go straight for two minutes. -> Jalan lurus selama dua menit.
- **Dimas:** Okay. Go straight for two minutes. -> Oke. Jalan lurus selama dua menit.
- **Mina:** Then turn right at the bank. -> Lalu belok kanan di bank.
- **Dimas:** Turn right at the bank? -> Belok kanan di bank?
- **Mina:** Yes. The station is there. -> Ya. Stasiunnya di sana.
- **Dimas:** Thank you for your help. -> Terima kasih atas bantuanmu.
