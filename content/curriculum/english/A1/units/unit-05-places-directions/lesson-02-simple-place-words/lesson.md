# Simple Place Words

## Conversation Goal

Learners can name common places and answer where someone is going.

## Situation Setup

Kamu bicara dengan teman setelah kelas. Kamu menyebut tempat sederhana yang ingin kamu datangi.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada pertanyaan tempat, arah sederhana, dan cara memastikan informasi.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Start politely.
2. Ask where the place is or how to get there.
3. Listen for place words, floor, landmark, or direction steps.
4. Repeat or confirm the key direction.
5. Close with thanks.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "I'm going to the cafe."
