# Grammar for Conversation: Where is...?

Grammar di lesson ini hanya dipakai untuk membantu percakapan, bukan untuk hafalan panjang.

## Use 'Where is + place?'

- Example: Where is the classroom?

## Answer with 'It is...'

- Example: It is on the first floor.

## Use 'near' and 'next to' for simple location.

- Example: It is next to the office.
