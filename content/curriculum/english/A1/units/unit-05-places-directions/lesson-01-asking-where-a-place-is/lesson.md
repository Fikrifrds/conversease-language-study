# Asking Where a Place Is

## Conversation Goal

Learners can ask where a place is and understand a short location answer.

## Situation Setup

Kamu berada di gedung kursus baru. Kamu perlu mencari ruang kelas dan bertanya dengan sopan.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada pertanyaan tempat, arah sederhana, dan cara memastikan informasi.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Start politely.
2. Ask where the place is or how to get there.
3. Listen for place words, floor, landmark, or direction steps.
4. Repeat or confirm the key direction.
5. Close with thanks.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Where is the classroom?"
