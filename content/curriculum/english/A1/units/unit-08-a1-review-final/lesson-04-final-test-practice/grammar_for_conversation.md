# Grammar for Conversation: reviewing complete short answers

Grammar di lesson ini membantu learner menggabungkan pola A1 yang sudah dipelajari.

## Use complete A1 answers in a test.

- Example: My name is Alya. I'm from Indonesia.

## Use every morning for routine frequency.

- Example: I study English every morning.

## Use can you repeat for clarification.

- Example: Sorry, can you repeat that, please?
