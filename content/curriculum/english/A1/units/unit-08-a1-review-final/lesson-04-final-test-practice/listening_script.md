# Dialogue Script

**Sara:** Hello. What is your name?
**Alya:** My name is Alya.
**Sara:** Where are you from?
**Alya:** I'm from Indonesia.
**Sara:** What do you do every morning?
**Alya:** I study English at seven.
**Sara:** Good. When is your class?
**Alya:** Sorry, can you repeat that, please?
**Sara:** Sure. When is your class?
**Alya:** It is on Tuesday at eight.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: review, clear, supportive
- Voices: female examiner and female learner, clear final-test pace with short pauses
