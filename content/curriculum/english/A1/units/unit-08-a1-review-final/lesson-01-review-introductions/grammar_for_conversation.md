# Grammar for Conversation: reviewing be verbs for introductions

Grammar di lesson ini membantu learner menggabungkan pola A1 yang sudah dipelajari.

## Use I am or I'm for names.

- Example: I'm Sara. My name is Dimas.

## Use from for origin.

- Example: I'm from Indonesia.

## Use live in for current city.

- Example: I live in Jakarta now.
