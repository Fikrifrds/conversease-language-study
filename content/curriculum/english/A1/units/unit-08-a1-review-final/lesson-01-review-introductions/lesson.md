# Review Introductions

## Conversation Goal

Learners can greet someone, introduce themselves, say where they are from, and ask simple follow-up questions.

## Situation Setup

Ini review awal sebelum final A1. Kamu bertemu teman kelas baru dan merapikan pola greeting, name, origin, dan closing.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog review ini. Fokus pada cara menggabungkan skill A1 lama dalam percakapan yang tetap pendek dan natural.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Open with a greeting.
2. Say your name clearly.
3. Say where you are from or where you live.
4. Ask the same question back.
5. Close politely.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Hi, my name is Dimas. I'm from Indonesia. How about you?"
