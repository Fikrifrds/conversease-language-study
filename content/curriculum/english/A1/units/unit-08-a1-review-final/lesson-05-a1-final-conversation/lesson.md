# A1 Final Conversation

## Conversation Goal

Learners can complete a full A1 conversation with greetings, identity, routine, places, ordering, help language, and polite closing.

## Situation Setup

Ini misi final A1. Kamu melakukan percakapan lebih panjang, tetapi kalimat tetap pendek, jelas, dan sesuai level A1.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog review ini. Fokus pada cara menggabungkan skill A1 lama dalam percakapan yang tetap pendek dan natural.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Greet and introduce yourself.
2. Answer origin and routine questions.
3. Ask for a place and follow directions.
4. Order one item and ask the price.
5. Ask for help or repetition and close politely.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "I can introduce myself, ask simple questions, order one item, ask for help, and close politely."
