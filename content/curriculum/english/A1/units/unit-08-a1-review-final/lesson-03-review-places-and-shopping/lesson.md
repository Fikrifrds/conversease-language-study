# Review Places and Shopping

## Conversation Goal

Learners can ask where a place is, follow simple directions, order one item, and ask the price.

## Situation Setup

Kamu mencari cafe dekat kelas, lalu membeli satu minuman. Ini menggabungkan places, directions, ordering, dan prices.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog review ini. Fokus pada cara menggabungkan skill A1 lama dalam percakapan yang tetap pendek dan natural.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Ask where a place is.
2. Understand two short directions.
3. Order one item.
4. Ask the price.
5. Pay and thank the cashier.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Where is the cafe? I would like one tea, please. How much is it?"
