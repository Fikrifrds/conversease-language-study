# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Excuse me. Where is the cafe?
**Ben:** Go straight and turn right.
**Faris:** Is it next to the library?
**Ben:** Yes, it is. I work there. Come with me.
**Faris:** Thank you.
**Ben:** Here we are. <#0.8#> Welcome to the cafe. What would you like?
**Faris:** Hi. I would like one tea, please.
**Ben:** Sure. It is two dollars.
**Faris:** Here you go. Thank you.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: review, clear, supportive
- Voices: female customer and male helper or cashier, natural but slow service tone
