# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** Excuse me. Where is the cafe?
**Ben:** Go straight and turn right.
**Mina:** Is it next to the library?
**Ben:** Yes, it is.
**Mina:** Thank you.
**Ben:** You're welcome.
**Mina:** Hi. I would like one tea, please.
**Ben:** Sure. It is two dollars.
**Mina:** Here you go. Thank you.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: review, clear, supportive
- Voices: female customer and male helper or cashier, natural but slow service tone
