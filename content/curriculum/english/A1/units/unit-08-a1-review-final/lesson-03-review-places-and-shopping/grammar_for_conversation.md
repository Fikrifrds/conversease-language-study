# Grammar for Conversation: reviewing where, there is, and would like

Grammar di lesson ini membantu learner menggabungkan pola A1 yang sudah dipelajari.

## Use Where is for one place.

- Example: Where is the cafe?

## Use imperatives for directions.

- Example: Go straight. Turn right.

## Use I would like for polite orders.

- Example: I would like one tea, please.
