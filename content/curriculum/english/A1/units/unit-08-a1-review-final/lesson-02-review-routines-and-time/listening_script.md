# Dialogue Script

**Alya:** What do you do in the morning?
**Ben:** I wake up at six.
**Alya:** Do you study English every day?
**Ben:** Yes, I study English at seven.
**Alya:** When is our speaking class?
**Ben:** It is on Tuesday at eight.
**Alya:** Great. See you on Tuesday.
**Ben:** See you then.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: review, clear, supportive
- Voices: female classmate and male learner, slow and clear time expressions
