# Dialogue Script

**Dimas:** What do you do in the morning?
**Ben:** I wake up at six.
**Dimas:** Nice. Do you study English after that?
**Ben:** Yes, I study English at seven.
**Dimas:** Good. We have speaking class this week.
**Ben:** When is our speaking class?
**Dimas:** It is on Tuesday at eight.
**Ben:** Great. See you then.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: review, clear, supportive
- Voices: female classmate and male learner, slow and clear time expressions
