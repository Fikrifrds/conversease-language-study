# Review Routines and Time

## Conversation Goal

Learners can talk about a simple daily routine, class day, and class time in one short conversation.

## Situation Setup

Kamu ngobrol dengan teman kelas tentang rutinitas pagi dan jadwal kelas. Fokus pada time, days, dan simple present.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog review ini. Fokus pada cara menggabungkan skill A1 lama dalam percakapan yang tetap pendek dan natural.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Say one daily routine.
2. Say the time.
3. Hear the class context.
4. Ask when the class is.
5. Close after the day and time are clear.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "I study English at seven. When is our speaking class? It is on Tuesday at eight."
