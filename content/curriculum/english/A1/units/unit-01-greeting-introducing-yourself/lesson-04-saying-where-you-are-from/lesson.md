# Saying Where You Are From

## Conversation Goal

Learner can say where they are from, say where they live, and ask the same question back.

## Situation Setup

Kamu melanjutkan perkenalan singkat. Jawab asalmu, lalu tanyakan balik agar percakapan berjalan.

## Dialogue

- Coach: Where are you from?
- Learner: I'm from Indonesia.
- Coach: Oh, nice.
- Learner: How about you?
