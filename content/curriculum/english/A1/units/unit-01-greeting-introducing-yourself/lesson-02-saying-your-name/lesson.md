# Saying Your Name

## Conversation Goal

Learner can say their name clearly, respond to an introduction, and use a simple nickname phrase.

## Situation Setup

Kamu masuk kelas online dan diminta memperkenalkan nama dengan kalimat pendek yang jelas.

## Dialogue

- Coach: Hi, my name is Sara.
- Learner: Hi Sara. My name is Arif.
- Coach: Nice to meet you, Arif.
- Learner: Nice to meet you too.

## Teaching Notes

Keep the first response short. The goal is clarity, not a long biography.
