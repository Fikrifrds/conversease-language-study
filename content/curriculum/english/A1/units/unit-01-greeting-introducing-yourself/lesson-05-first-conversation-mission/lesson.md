# First Conversation Mission

## Conversation Goal

Learner can combine greeting, name, origin, a follow-up question, and a polite closing.

## Situation Setup

Ini misi gabungan Unit 1. Selesaikan percakapan pendek tanpa melihat contoh terlalu sering.

## Dialogue

- Learner: Hi, good morning. My name is Arif.
- Coach: Good morning, Arif. Where are you from?
- Learner: I'm from Indonesia. How about you?
- Coach: I'm from Malaysia. Nice to meet you.
- Learner: Nice to meet you too. See you later.
