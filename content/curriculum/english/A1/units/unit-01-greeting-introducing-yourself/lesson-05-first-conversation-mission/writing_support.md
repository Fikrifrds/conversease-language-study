# Writing Support

Write a six-line first conversation:

1. A greeting.
2. Your name.
3. Your country or city.
4. Ask the other person's name.
5. Ask where the person is from.
6. Close the conversation.

Example:

```txt
Hi.
My name is Fikri.
I'm from Indonesia.
What is your name?
Where are you from?
See you later.
```
