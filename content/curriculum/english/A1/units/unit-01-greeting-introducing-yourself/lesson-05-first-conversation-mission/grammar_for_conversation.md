# Grammar for Conversation

Use short A1 patterns together to make one conversation.

Examples:

- My name is Raka.
- I'm Adi.
- I'm from Indonesia.
- What is your name?
- Where are you from?

Patterns:

```txt
My name is + name
I'm + name
I'm from + place
What is your name?
Where are you from?
```

Use one idea per sentence. This keeps the conversation clear.
