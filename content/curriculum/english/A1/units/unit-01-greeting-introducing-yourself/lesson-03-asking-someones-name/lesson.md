# Asking Someone's Name

## Conversation Goal

Learner can ask for someone's name, confirm it, and continue with a polite greeting.

## Situation Setup

Kamu bertemu peserta baru di kelas. Tanyakan namanya, lalu ulangi nama itu dengan sopan.

## Dialogue

- Learner: Hi. What's your name?
- Mina: My name is Mina.
- Learner: Nice to meet you, Mina.
- Mina: Nice to meet you too.
