# Dialogue Script

**Adi:** Hello. What's your name?
**Raka:** My name is Raka.
**Adi:** Sorry, can you repeat that?
**Raka:** Raka. R-A-K-A.
**Adi:** Thank you, Raka.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: polite and supportive
- Voices: two adults, clear pronunciation
