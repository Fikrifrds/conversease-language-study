# Dialogue Script

**Dimas:** What do you do in the morning?
**Ben:** I wake up at six.
**Dimas:** What do you do after that?
**Ben:** I study English at seven.
**Dimas:** Nice. Do you work in the afternoon?
**Ben:** Yes, I work at one.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female learner, one male learner
