# Dialogue Script

**Dimas:** What time is the class?
**Ben:** It's at nine o'clock.
**Dimas:** In the morning?
**Ben:** Yes, in the morning.
**Dimas:** Thank you.
**Ben:** You're welcome.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female learner, one male learner
