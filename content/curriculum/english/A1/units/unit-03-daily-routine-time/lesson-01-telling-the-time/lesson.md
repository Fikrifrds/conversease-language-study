# Telling the Time

## Conversation Outcome

After this lesson, learners can ask and answer a simple question about time.

## Situation

Kamu ingin tahu jam mulai kelas online. Tanyakan waktunya, lalu cek apakah itu pagi, siang, atau malam.

## Lesson Flow

1. Listen to a short dialogue.
2. Understand the conversation with Indonesian support.
3. Practice useful time and routine phrases.
4. Repeat key phrases clearly.
5. Respond to simple prompts.
6. Practice the same situation with Conversation Coach.
7. Review conversation feedback.
