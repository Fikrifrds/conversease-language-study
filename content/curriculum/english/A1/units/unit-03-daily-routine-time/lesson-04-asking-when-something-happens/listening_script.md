# Dialogue Script

**Dimas:** When is the meeting?
**Alya:** It's tomorrow at ten.
**Dimas:** Is it online?
**Alya:** Yes, it is online.
**Dimas:** Tomorrow at ten. Is that right?
**Alya:** Yes, that's right.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one male learner, one female learner
