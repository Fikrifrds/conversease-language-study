# Dialogue Script

**Dimas:** Hi, Ben. What time do you wake up?
**Ben:** I wake up at six.
**Dimas:** When do you study English?
**Ben:** I study on Monday and Wednesday at seven.
**Dimas:** Is the class online?
**Ben:** Yes, it is online.
**Dimas:** Great. See you on Monday.
**Ben:** See you.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female learner, one male learner
