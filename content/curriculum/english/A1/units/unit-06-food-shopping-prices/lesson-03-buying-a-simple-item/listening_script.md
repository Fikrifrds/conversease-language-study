# Dialogue Script

**Ben:** Excuse me. Can I have this pen?
**Alya:** Yes, of course.
**Ben:** How much is it?
**Alya:** It is one dollar.
**Ben:** Okay. Here you go.
**Alya:** Thank you. Here is your pen.
**Ben:** Thanks.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, practical
- Voices: one male learner, one female shop staff
