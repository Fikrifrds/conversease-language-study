# Buying a Simple Item

## Conversation Goal

Learners can buy one simple item, ask the price, and complete the payment.

## Situation Setup

Kamu berada di toko alat tulis. Kamu membeli satu pulpen dan membayar dengan sopan.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada cara memesan, bertanya harga, memilih opsi, dan menutup transaksi dengan sopan.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Start politely.
2. Say what you want or order.
3. Answer a simple option question.
4. Ask or confirm the price.
5. Pay and close with thanks.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Can I have this pen?"
