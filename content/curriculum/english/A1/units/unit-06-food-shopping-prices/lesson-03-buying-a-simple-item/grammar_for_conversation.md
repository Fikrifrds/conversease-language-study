# Grammar for Conversation: this + item

Grammar di lesson ini dipakai untuk menyelesaikan transaksi sederhana, bukan untuk hafalan panjang.

## Use 'this' for one item near you.

- Example: Can I have this pen?

## Use 'it' when the item is already known.

- Example: How much is it?

## Use 'Here is + item' for one item.

- Example: Here is your pen.
