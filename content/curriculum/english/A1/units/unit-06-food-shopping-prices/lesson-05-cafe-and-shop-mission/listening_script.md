# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hi! (chuckle) What would you like?
**Ben:** Can I have a coffee and a sandwich, please?
**Faris:** Sure. Small or large coffee?
**Ben:** Small, please. How much is it?
**Faris:** It is five dollars.
**Ben:** Okay. Here you go.
**Faris:** Thank you. Here is your order.
**Ben:** Thanks.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, practical
- Voices: one female cafe staff, one male learner
