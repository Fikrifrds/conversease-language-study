# Cafe and Shop Mission

## Conversation Goal

Learners can order, ask a price, choose an option, and complete a simple purchase.

## Situation Setup

Ini misi akhir unit. Kamu memesan minuman dan makanan kecil, bertanya harga, memilih opsi, lalu membayar.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada cara memesan, bertanya harga, memilih opsi, dan menutup transaksi dengan sopan.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Start politely.
2. Say what you want or order.
3. Answer a simple option question.
4. Ask or confirm the price.
5. Pay and close with thanks.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Can I have a coffee and a sandwich, please?"
