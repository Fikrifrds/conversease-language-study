# Grammar for Conversation: combining order and price language

Grammar di lesson ini dipakai untuk menyelesaikan transaksi sederhana, bukan untuk hafalan panjang.

## Use 'and' to order two items.

- Example: a coffee and a sandwich

## Use 'How much is it?' for the total.

- Example: How much is it?

## Use 'Here is...' for one order.

- Example: Here is your order.
