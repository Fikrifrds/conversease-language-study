# Ordering a Drink

## Conversation Goal

Learners can order one drink politely and answer a simple size question.

## Situation Setup

Kamu berada di kafe kecil setelah kelas. Pesan satu minuman dengan sopan dan jawab pertanyaan ukuran.

## Listen First

Dengarkan dialog pendek. Fokus pada cara memesan, bertanya harga, memilih opsi, dan menutup transaksi dengan sopan.

## Key Conversation Flow

1. Start politely.
2. Say what you want or order.
3. Answer a simple option question.
4. Ask or confirm the price.
5. Pay and close with thanks.

## Practice Target

By the end, you should be able to say: "Can I have a tea, please?"
