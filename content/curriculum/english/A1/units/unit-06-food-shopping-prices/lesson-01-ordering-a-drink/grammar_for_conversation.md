# Grammar for Conversation: Can I have...?

Grammar di lesson ini dipakai untuk menyelesaikan transaksi sederhana, bukan untuk hafalan panjang.

## Use 'Can I have + item, please?'

- Example: Can I have a tea, please?

## Use short size answers.

- Example: Small, please.

## Use 'No, thank you' to finish politely.

- Example: No, thank you.
