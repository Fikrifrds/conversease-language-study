# Dialogue Script

**Faris:** Hi! (chuckle) What would you like?
**Ben:** Can I have a tea, please?
**Faris:** Small or large?
**Ben:** Small, please.
**Faris:** Anything else?
**Ben:** No, thank you.
**Faris:** Here you go.
**Ben:** Thank you.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, practical
- Voices: one female cafe staff, one male learner
