# Grammar for Conversation: How much is...?

Grammar di lesson ini dipakai untuk menyelesaikan transaksi sederhana, bukan untuk hafalan panjang.

## Use 'How much is + item?'

- Example: How much is the coffee?

## Answer with 'It is + price.'

- Example: It is twenty thousand rupiah.

## Repeat a price to confirm.

- Example: Twenty thousand rupiah?
