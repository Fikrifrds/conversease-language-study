# Transcript Translation

- **Sara:** What do you want? -> Kamu mau apa?
- **Dimas:** I want a sandwich. -> Saya mau sandwich.
- **Sara:** Do you want tea or coffee? -> Kamu mau teh atau kopi?
- **Dimas:** Tea, please. -> Teh, tolong.
- **Sara:** Do you want sugar? -> Kamu mau gula?
- **Dimas:** No sugar, please. -> Tanpa gula, tolong.
- **Sara:** Okay. -> Oke.
