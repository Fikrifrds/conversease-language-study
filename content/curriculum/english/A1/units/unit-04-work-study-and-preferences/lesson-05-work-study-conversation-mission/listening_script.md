# Dialogue Script

**Mina:** Hi, Ben. Do you work or study?
**Ben:** I study English online and I work in a shop.
**Mina:** What do you like about English?
**Ben:** I like speaking practice.
**Mina:** What can you do in English?
**Ben:** I can introduce myself and write simple messages.
**Mina:** Great. Keep practicing.
**Ben:** Thank you. I will.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female learner, one male learner
