# Dialogue Script

**Alya:** Do you like English?
**Ben:** Yes, I like it.
**Alya:** What do you like?
**Ben:** I like speaking practice.
**Alya:** Do you like grammar?
**Ben:** It's okay, but speaking is my favorite.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female learner, one male learner
