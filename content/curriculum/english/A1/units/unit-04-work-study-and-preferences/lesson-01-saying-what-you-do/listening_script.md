# Dialogue Script

**Omar:** What do you do?
**Dimas:** I'm a student.
**Omar:** What do you study?
**Dimas:** I study design.
**Omar:** Do you study online?
**Dimas:** Yes, I study online.

## Audio Direction

- Level: A1
- Speed: slow and natural
- Tone: friendly, clear, supportive
- Voices: one female learner, one male learner
