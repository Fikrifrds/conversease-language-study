# Arabic C1 - Advanced Fluency

## Level Outcome

Learners can participate in complex professional and social conversations with nuance, precision, tact, strategic framing, persuasive structure, and accurate response to indirect or dense speech in formal Arabic.

## Scope

- Formal Arabic / Modern Standard Arabic, not dialect.
- Indonesian explanations for adult learners.
- Arabic script with harakat for learner-facing examples and listening scripts.
- Complex but practical conversation targets: nuanced opinions, strategic work communication, advanced presentations, debate, cross-cultural professionalism, leadership, and advanced listening response.
- Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken in generated audio.
- No religious source text or devotional TTS content.

## C1 Upgrade From B2

- Dialogue length: 8 dense turns with explicit nuance and repair strategies.
- Learner responses include qualification, uncertainty, counterpoint, tact, and precise final formulation.
- Introduce advanced discourse markers and indirect meaning handling.
- Keep language formal, natural, and usable for professional conversation.

## Passing Threshold

- Overall score: 82
- Speaking / Conversation: 76
- Listening: 74
- Pronunciation: 68
- Vocabulary / Useful Phrases: 74
- Grammar: 74
- Reading: 70
- Writing: 70
- Lesson completion: 90%
