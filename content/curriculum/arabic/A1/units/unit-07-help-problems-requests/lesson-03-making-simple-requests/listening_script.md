# Listening Script

**Muallim:** اِفْتَحِ الْكِتَابَ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ.
**Layla:** نَعَمْ، أَفْتَحُ الْكِتَابَ.
**Muallim:** اُكْتُبِي الْجُمْلَةَ.
**Layla:** أَكْتُبُ الْجُمْلَةَ الْآنَ.
**Muallim:** اِسْتَمِعِي ثُمَّ تَكَلَّمِي.
**Layla:** حَسَنًا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
