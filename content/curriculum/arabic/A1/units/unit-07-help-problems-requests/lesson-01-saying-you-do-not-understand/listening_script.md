# Listening Script

**Maryam:** اِقرأِ الجملةَ.
**Ahmad:** لَا أَفْهَمُ الْكَلِمَةَ.
**Maryam:** أي كلمة؟
**Ahmad:** مَاذَا يَعْنِي هَذَا؟
**Maryam:** هَذِهِ الْكَلِمَةُ تَعْنِي: كِتَابٌ.
**Ahmad:** شُكْرًا، الْآنَ أفهم.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
