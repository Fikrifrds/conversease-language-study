# Listening Script

**Khalid:** مَاذَا تَعْمَلُ؟
**Zayd:** أَنَا طَالِبٌ.
**Khalid:** أَيْنَ تَدْرُسُ؟
**Zayd:** أَدْرُسُ فِي مَدْرَسَةٍ.
**Khalid:** مَاذَا تَدْرُسُ؟
**Zayd:** أَدْرُسُ الْعَرَبِيَّةَ.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
