# Listening Script

**Zayd:** مَنْ هَذَا؟
**Ahmad:** هَذَا أَبِي.
**Zayd:** وَمَنْ هَذِهِ؟
**Ahmad:** هَذِهِ أُمِّي.
**Zayd:** هل هَذِهِ عَائِلَتُكَ؟
**Ahmad:** نَعَمْ، هَذِهِ عَائِلَتِي.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
