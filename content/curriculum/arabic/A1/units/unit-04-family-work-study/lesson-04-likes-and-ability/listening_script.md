# Listening Script

**Ahmad:** مَاذَا تُحِبِّينَ؟
**Noura:** أُحِبُّ الْقِرَاءَةَ.
**Ahmad:** هَلْ تَسْتَطِيعِينَ الْكِتَابَةَ بِالْعَرَبِيَّةِ؟
**Noura:** أَسْتَطِيعُ الْكِتَابَةَ قَلِيلًا.
**Ahmad:** جَيِّدٌ جِدًّا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
