# Listening Script

**Muallim:** كَيْفَ تَكْتُبُ اِسْمَكَ؟
**Ahmad:** أَكْتُبُ اسْمِي: أَحْمَدُ.
**Muallim:** هَذَا حَرْفُ الْأَلِفِ، وَهَذَا حَرْفُ الْحَاءِ.
**Ahmad:** هَلْ هَذَا صَحِيحٌ؟
**Muallim:** نَعَمْ، هَذَا صَحِيحٌ.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
