# Listening Script

**Layla:** لم أسمع الحرف.
**Zayd:** الْحَرْفُ بَاءٌ.
**Layla:** بِبُطْءٍ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ.
**Zayd:** بَاءٌ.
**Layla:** هَلْ قُلْتَ بَاءً؟
**Zayd:** نَعَمْ، قلت بَاءٌ.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
