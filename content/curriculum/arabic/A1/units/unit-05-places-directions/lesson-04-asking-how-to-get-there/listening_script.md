# Listening Script

**Ahmad:** كَيْفَ أَذْهَبُ إِلَى الْمَقْهَى؟
**Maryam:** امشِ إِلَى الْأَمَامِ.
**Ahmad:** ثُمَّ مَاذَا؟
**Maryam:** ثُمَّ اِذْهَبْ يَسَارًا عِنْدَ الْبَابِ.
**Ahmad:** شُكْرًا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
