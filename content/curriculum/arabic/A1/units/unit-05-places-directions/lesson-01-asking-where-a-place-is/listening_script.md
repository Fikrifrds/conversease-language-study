# Listening Script

**Zayd:** أَيْنَ الْمَكْتَبَةُ؟
**Karim:** الْمَكْتَبَةُ هُنَاكَ.
**Zayd:** هَلْ هِيَ قَرِيبَةٌ؟
**Karim:** نَعَمْ، هِيَ قَرِيبَةٌ.
**Zayd:** شُكْرًا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
