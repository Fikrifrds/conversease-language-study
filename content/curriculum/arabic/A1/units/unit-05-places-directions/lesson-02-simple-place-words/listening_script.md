# Listening Script

**Zayd:** أَيْنَ الْمَدْرَسَةُ؟
**Ahmad:** الْمَدْرَسَةُ قَرِيبَةٌ.
**Zayd:** وَأَيْنَ السُّوقُ؟
**Ahmad:** السُّوقُ بَعِيدٌ.
**Zayd:** هَلِ الْمَقْهَى هُنَا؟
**Ahmad:** نَعَمْ، الْمَقْهَى هُنَا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
