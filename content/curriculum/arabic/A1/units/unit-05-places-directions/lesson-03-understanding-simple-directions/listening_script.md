# Listening Script

**Khalid:** كَيْفَ أَذْهَبُ إِلَى الْمَكْتَبَةِ؟
**Layla:** اِذْهَبْ إِلَى الْأَمَامِ.
**Khalid:** ثُمَّ؟
**Layla:** ثُمَّ اِذْهَبْ يَمِينًا.
**Khalid:** أَيْنَ الْمَكْتَبَةُ؟
**Layla:** هِيَ بِجَانِبِ الْمَكْتَبِ.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
