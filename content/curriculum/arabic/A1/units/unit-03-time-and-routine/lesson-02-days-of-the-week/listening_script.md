# Listening Script

**Noura:** أي يَوْمَ الْيَوْمُ؟
**Ahmad:** الْيَوْمُ الْإِثْنَيْنِ.
**Noura:** هَلْ عِنْدَكَ دَرْسٌ غَدًا؟
**Ahmad:** نَعَمْ، عِنْدِي دَرْسٌ يَوْمَ الثُّلَاثَاءِ.
**Noura:** جَيِّدٌ.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
