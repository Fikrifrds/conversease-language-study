# Listening Script

**Khalid:** كَمِ السَّاعَةُ الْآنَ؟
**Karim:** السَّاعَةُ الثَّامِنَةُ صَبَاحًا.
**Khalid:** مَتَى الدَّرْسُ؟
**Karim:** الدَّرْسُ السَّاعَةَ التَّاسِعَةَ.
**Khalid:** شُكْرًا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
