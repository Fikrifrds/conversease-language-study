# Listening Script

**Muallim:** مَا اسْمُكَ؟
**Ahmad:** اِسْمِي أَحْمَدُ.
**Muallim:** مِنْ أَيْنَ أَنْتَ؟
**Ahmad:** أَنَا مِنْ إِنْدُونِيسِيَا. وَأَنْتَ؟
**Muallim:** أَنَا مِنَ الْمَمْلَكَةِ الْعَرَبِيَّةِ السُّعُودِيَّةِ.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Assign different curated male ElevenLabs voices to Muallim and Ahmad. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
