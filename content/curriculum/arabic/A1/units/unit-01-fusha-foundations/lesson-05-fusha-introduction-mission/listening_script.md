# Listening Script

**Muallim:** مَرْحَبًا، مَا اسْمُكَ؟
**Ahmad:** مَرْحَبًا، اِسْمِي أَحْمَدُ.
**Muallim:** مِنْ أَيْنَ أَنْتَ؟
**Ahmad:** أَنَا مِنْ إِنْدُونِيسِيَا.
**Muallim:** اِقْرَأِ الْجُمْلَةَ.
**Ahmad:** عُفْوًا، لَا أَفْهَمُ. أَعِدْ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ.
**Muallim:** حَسَنًا، أَقْرَؤُهَا مَرَّةً أُخْرَى.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Assign different curated male ElevenLabs voices to Muallim and Ahmad. Keep pacing formal, calm, and beginner-friendly with a short natural pause between speakers.
