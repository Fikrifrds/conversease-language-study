# Listening Script

**Muallim:** مَرْحَبًا.
**Khalid:** أَهْلًا وَسَهْلًا.
**Muallim:** كَيْفَ حَالُكَ الْيَوْمَ؟
**Khalid:** أَنَا بِخَيْرٍ، شُكْرًا.
**Muallim:** أَحْسَنْتَ.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Assign a curated male ElevenLabs voice to Muallim and a curated male ElevenLabs voice to Khalid. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
