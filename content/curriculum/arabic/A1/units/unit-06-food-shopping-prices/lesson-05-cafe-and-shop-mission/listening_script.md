# Listening Script

**Muallimah:** مَاذَا تُرِيدُ؟
**Zayd:** أُرِيدُ مَاءً مِنْ فَضْلِكَ.
**Muallimah:** تَفَضَّلْ.
**Zayd:** كَمِ السِّعْرُ؟
**Muallimah:** السِّعْرُ رِيَالَانِ.
**Zayd:** آخُذُ وَاحِدًا، شُكْرًا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
