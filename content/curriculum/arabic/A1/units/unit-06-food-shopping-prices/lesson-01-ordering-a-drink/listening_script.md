# Listening Script

**Khalid:** مَرْحَبًا.
**Cafe Staff:** مَرْحَبًا، مَاذَا تُرِيدُ؟
**Khalid:** أُرِيدُ مَاءً مِنْ فَضْلِكَ.
**Cafe Staff:** تَفَضَّلْ.
**Khalid:** شُكْرًا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
