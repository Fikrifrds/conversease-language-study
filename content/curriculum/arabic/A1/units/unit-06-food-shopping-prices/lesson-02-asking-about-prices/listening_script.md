# Listening Script

**Khalid:** كَمْ سِعْرُ الْقَلَمِ؟
**Shopkeeper:** سِعْرُهُ خَمْسَةُ رِيَالَاتٍ.
**Khalid:** السِّعْرُ مُنَاسِبٌ.
**Shopkeeper:** هَلْ تَأْخُذُ وَاحِدًا؟
**Khalid:** نَعَمْ، آخُذُ وَاحِدًا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
