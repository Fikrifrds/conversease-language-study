# Listening Script

**Maryam:** كَمْ سِعْرُ الْقَلَمِ؟
**Zayd:** سِعْرُهُ خَمْسَةُ رِيَالَاتٍ.
**Maryam:** السِّعْرُ مُنَاسِبٌ.
**Zayd:** هَلْ تَأْخُذِينَ وَاحِدًا؟
**Maryam:** نَعَمْ، آخُذُ وَاحِدًا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
