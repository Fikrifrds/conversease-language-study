# Listening Script

**Shopkeeper:** مَاذَا تُرِيدُ؟
**Khalid:** أُرِيدُ كِتَابًا.
**Shopkeeper:** هَلْ تُرِيدُ قَهْوَةً أَيْضًا؟
**Khalid:** لَا أُرِيدُ قَهْوَةً.
**Shopkeeper:** هَلْ تَحْتَاجُ شَيْئًا آخَرَ؟
**Khalid:** أَحْتَاجُ قَلَمًا فَقَطْ.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
