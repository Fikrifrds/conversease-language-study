# Listening Script

**Ahmad:** هَلْ عِنْدَكُمْ قَلَمٌ؟
**Shopkeeper:** نَعَمْ، مَوْجُودٌ.
**Ahmad:** أُرِيدُ هَذَا.
**Shopkeeper:** كَمْ قَلَمًا تُرِيدُ؟
**Ahmad:** آخُذُ وَاحِدًا.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers.
