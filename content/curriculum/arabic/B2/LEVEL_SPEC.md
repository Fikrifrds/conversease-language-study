# Arabic B2 - Professional Discussions

## Level Outcome

Learners can participate in structured professional and social discussions: state a position, support it with reasons and examples, respond to objections, take part in meetings, negotiate compromises, present ideas, evaluate sources, communicate with clients, and recommend solutions in clear formal Arabic.

## Scope

- Formal Arabic / Modern Standard Arabic, not dialect.
- Indonesian explanations for adult learners.
- Arabic script with harakat for learner-facing examples and listening scripts.
- Discussion-first lessons with explicit argument, meeting, and problem-solving patterns.
- Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken in generated audio.
- No religious source text or devotional TTS content.

## B2 Upgrade From B1

- Dialogue length: 8 connected turns.
- Learner responses include position, reason, example, counterpoint response, and recommendation.
- Introduce professional discussion, negotiation, presentation, source evaluation, and tradeoffs.
- Keep grammar functional and conversation-first.

## Passing Threshold

- Overall score: 78
- Speaking / Conversation: 72
- Listening: 70
- Pronunciation: 64
- Vocabulary / Useful Phrases: 70
- Grammar: 70
- Reading: 65
- Writing: 65
- Lesson completion: 85%
