# Listening Script

**Noura:** مَرْحَبًا، أَبْحَثُ عَنْ قَلَمٍ أَزْرَقَ.
**Karim:** نَعَمْ، عِنْدَنَا هَذَا الْقَلَمُ.
**Noura:** هَلْ يُوجَدُ لَوْنٌ آخَرُ؟
**Karim:** عِنْدَنَا أَزْرَقُ وَأَسْوَدُ وَأَحْمَرُ.
**Noura:** سَآخُذُ الْأَزْرَقَ، مِنْ فَضْلِكَ.
**Karim:** تَفَضَّلِي.

## Audio Direction

Use Arabic only. Speaker labels are metadata and must not be spoken. Keep a calm pace with a short natural pause between speakers. Use distinct male/female voices according to speaker names.
