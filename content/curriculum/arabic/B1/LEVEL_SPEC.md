# Arabic B1 - Connected Conversations

## Level Outcome

Learners can tell connected personal stories, handle work and travel situations, explain problems and solutions, compare options, discuss goals, and ask polite cultural questions in clear formal Arabic.

## Scope

- Formal Arabic / Modern Standard Arabic, not dialect.
- Indonesian explanations for adult learners.
- Arabic script with harakat for learner-facing examples and listening scripts.
- Conversation-first lessons with reading and writing support.
- Connected answers with context, reason, result, and next step.
- No religious source text or devotional TTS content.

## B1 Upgrade From A2

- Dialogue length: 6-10 connected turns.
- Learner responses may combine context, action, reason, and result.
- Introduce story sequencing, clarification, updates, recommendations, comparison, agreement, and polite cultural questions.
- Keep vocabulary practical and concrete even when answers are longer.

## Passing Threshold

- Overall score: 75
- Speaking / Conversation: 70
- Listening: 68
- Pronunciation: 62
- Vocabulary / Useful Phrases: 68
- Grammar: 68
- Reading: 60
- Writing: 60
- Lesson completion: 80%
